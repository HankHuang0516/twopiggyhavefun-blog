<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">燒餅加蛋 $23元</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">外酥內嫩的口感，嘴裡都是芝麻香氣，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">裡面只有夾蛋，吃完一個會覺得位有點空虛，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">建議再加點飯糰，會比較有飽足感唷~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29712396587" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1877/29712396587_ca16d3cc7b_z.jpg" alt="IMG_4728"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/30780160658" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1895/30780160658_25aee153f1_z.jpg" alt="IMG_4729"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">飯糰 $27元</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">飯糰只有包著油條和豆酥~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">白飯粒粒分明，比較有嚼勁，需要多咬幾下!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">而裡頭的油條我很喜歡，吃起來相當酥脆!</span></p>
