<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #0000ff;">** 喜歡旅遊、愛吃美食嗎？歡迎加入&nbsp;</span><span style="color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; text-decoration: underline;"><a style="color: #000000; text-decoration: underline;" href="https://www.facebook.com/twopiggyhavefun/?fref=ts" target="_blank"><span style="color: #ff0000; text-decoration: underline;">兩隻小豬</span><span style="color: #ff0000; text-decoration: underline;">粉絲團</span></a></span><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #0000ff;">**</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><img title="page" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1456290320-2442992602.jpg" alt="page" border="0"> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #0000ff; background-color: #ffff00;"><strong><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px;">星上星港式飲茶(竹北店)</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">位於竹北市吳濁流路上的「星上星港式飲茶」，距離光明一路相當近</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">店面的周圍也有小小免費停車場，開車來也相當方便~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">店內用餐環境相當寬敞舒適，有提供四人座、六人座及多人座，適合各種大小聚會</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160220_222631_638" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1456281394-1343185947.jpg" alt="IMG_20160220_222631_638" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">「星上星港式飲茶」餐點選擇相當多元，有燒臘、海鮮類、煲仔飯、季節時蔬、腸粉、大中小點、飲品、甜點等，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">菜單價格算是滿平價的，而且口味或辣度都可以做調整唷!</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160220_222258_399" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1456281394-483962375.jpg" alt="IMG_20160220_222258_399" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">讓我驚訝的是，這裡茶資每個人才酌收9元(小朋友不收)，還不收服務費(過年期間會收10%)，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">一旁還有附設兒童遊戲區</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160220_222301_946" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1456281394-3236178700.jpg" alt="IMG_20160220_222301_946" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">桌上除了餐具之外，還有附上醬料、醬料碟、餐具等</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160220_222623_947" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1456281394-2299840472.jpg" alt="IMG_20160220_222623_947" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">熱茶可以選擇<span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #0000ff;">茉莉香片</span>或<span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #0000ff;">烏龍</span>，我們選的是<span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong>烏龍</strong></span>，好喝又解油膩</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">重點是<span style="font-size: 16px;">茶資</span>一個人才酌收9元(小朋友不收)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160220_222306_519" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1456281394-3446486957.jpg" alt="IMG_20160220_222306_519" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">點完餐準備開吃啦~~</span></p>
