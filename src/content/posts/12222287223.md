<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">已經好一陣子沒有來爬山了，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">放假一直吃吃吃感覺都變胖了，哈</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">所以就找小豬一起來爬「鶯歌石登山步道」</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">偶爾來個爆汗行程也不錯啦～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43101310124" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/858/43101310124_8287fb2ab8_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9271"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">鶯歌石登山步道</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">地址：新北市鶯歌區中正一路401號</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #993300;">（地址可定位在北鶯公園，再看指標從旁邊樓梯上去）</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">記得帶水、穿好走的鞋</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">-</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #0000ff;">🔹FB/痞客邦🔎兩隻小豬🐷🐷</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #0000ff;">🔹IG🔎twopiggyhavefun</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">通常登山口都會有很多個出口，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">我們ＧＯＯＧＬＥ地址定位在<span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #ff6600;">”北鶯公園”</span></span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42009315280" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1796/42009315280_fa15384d32_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9213"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這裡總共有１８條步道，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">有時間的話可以試試其他路線唷😎</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42009313950" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/848/42009313950_5b18871b3d_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9214"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">一條是往鶯歌石，另一條是通往孫龍步道！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42009312810" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/932/42009312810_ae26cf9fca_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9215"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">附近看起來是沒有停車場，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">騎車去會比較方便唷～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42009311100" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1836/42009311100_21bfe70229_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9216"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43817935711" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/858/43817935711_d864470d1e_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9217"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">走一小段路程後，會看到兩條分岔路</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">看到指標寫<span style="background-color: #ffff99;">”鶯歌石”</span>旁邊樓梯繼續走～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42009309860" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/850/42009309860_d2f534022d_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9218"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">據說明末清初之際，鄭成功領軍沿淡水河南下，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">到今三峽、鶯歌一帶突然遇到巨鳥吐霧，造成將兵傷亡，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">鄭軍即砲轟巨鳥，一擊中頸，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">巨鳥遂轟然落地，化為巨石匍匐於山腰，即今天所見之鶯歌石。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt; color: #008080;">（文字取自網路）</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42009307720" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/852/42009307720_e5a4526200_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9219"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">前半段路程都是階梯，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">讓我累的跟狗一樣</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">（平常有在爬山的小豬露出得意洋洋的表情😳）</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">幸好沿路都有樹蔭遮蔽，不至於太熱～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42009306410" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1772/42009306410_d2629c6de6_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9220"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42009304520" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/928/42009304520_1c5f6448c4_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9221"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">終於看到一個涼亭，可以喝個水休息一下！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42009302520" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1800/42009302520_53d16630dc_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9222"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">涼亭旁邊有一座岩石，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">岩石壁上的凹處放了土地公，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">感覺拍的時候一直反光，哈</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43101341594" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1820/43101341594_308bf1a2f6_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9223"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">休息完之後，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這段路程又是我討厭的樓梯（累）</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43101340544" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/941/43101340544_f344b3a3fb_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9224"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">走到鶯歌石旁邊就是觀景台，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">雖然高度不高，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但可以眺望鶯歌的街景！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42914123505" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/929/42914123505_0f1d2f1311_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9226"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43817937471" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/859/43817937471_5c27fb60fa_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9268"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">鶯歌的名稱由「鶯歌石」而來，這個石頭的模樣，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">由鶯歌火車站向北望去，就像一隻收翼的鶯歌鳥，因而得名。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">而就近鶯歌石後，可以發現在蜂巢式的洞穴裡，有疑似貝殼的遺蹟，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">並且風化的頗嚴重，值得地質學人士前往探索，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">視鶯歌石是否因地層變化，由海底隆起。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt; color: #008080;">（文字取自網路）</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29948380448" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/931/29948380448_bb715299a0_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9230"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29948379088" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/853/29948379088_efb29cbafd_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9231"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">阿朱發現岩石上有棵樹，好奇妙啊～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42914123075" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1767/42914123075_d3aace906d_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9229"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">再來，就是拍網美照時間！！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43817956751" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1774/43817956751_c72ef3ba32_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9232"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42914121345" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1839/42914121345_a362d2905d_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9234"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">光是走前面那段階梯，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">就覺得真是累爆了．．．</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但是望著遠方的山景、鶯歌街景，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">頓時覺得心曠神怡～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42914120205" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1773/42914120205_b59d43ac29_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9236"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43101315104" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/859/43101315104_cd8dda2c07_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9267"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">拍照到一半，有隻螳螂出現在小豬手上，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">大自然裡總是有許多小生物！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42914118655" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1816/42914118655_e09abe1516_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9245"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43101325604" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1793/43101325604_380cac4c73_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9246"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">接著，我們繼續往前行，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這一段都是陡峭的土徑，必須拉繩攀爬！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43101324804" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1836/43101324804_2219b18bc7_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9247"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43101323664" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/853/43101323664_9078f9ba43_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9249"></a></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">往前走，就會抵達第二個觀景台～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43101320264" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1818/43101320264_af540a5c72_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9255"></a></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">只是岩石上沒有縫隙，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">所以拍出來沒那麼逼真啦ＸＤ</span></p>
