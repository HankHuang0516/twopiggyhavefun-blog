<p><span style="font-family: arial; color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">這天因為工作的關係，忙到中午都沒時間吃飯，整天肚子極度餓<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/whatchutalkingabout_smile.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">台北馥敦飯店南京館位於南京東路上，捷運靠近南京三民站~</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">而港式飲茶在2樓的嘉園廳，用餐空間還算大，我們兩人坐在6人桌的圓桌~</span><br><span style="color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160304_220919_329" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1457322821-3289934106.jpg" alt="IMG_20160304_220919_329" border="0"> &nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">先介紹一下港式飲茶吃到飽的方案</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">目前是成人725+10%，兒童390+10%，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">假日時段每位需加收$100元</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160304_220915_806" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1457322887-1439364076.jpg" alt="IMG_20160304_220915_806" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">如果怕點太多吃不完，可以只單點一片或一顆，不怕浪費食物啦~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160304_220926_749" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1457322887-565781498.jpg" alt="IMG_20160304_220926_749" border="0"> &nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">熱茶有香片、烏龍、普洱可以選擇，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">不需另外付茶資，可以無限續壺唷</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">我們兩個混搭喝<strong>烏龍茶+普洱茶</strong>，味道烏龍茶偏重，好喝又解油膩~</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><img title="2016-03-04_23.10.16" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1457323191-309181117.jpg" alt="2016-03-04_23.10.16" border="0"> &nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><strong><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px;">油雞/海蜇皮</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">口感清爽的海蜇皮，味道微辣但還能接受</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">油雞肉質非常鮮嫩，不會太油膩</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160304_220922_860" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1457323712-3730855685.jpg" alt="IMG_20160304_220922_860" border="0"></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">河粉不是很好用筷子夾，夾的時候都黏在一塊，可能是煮好後放一陣子~</span></p>
