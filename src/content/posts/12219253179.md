<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">地址：台北市中山北路二段42巷38號</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">（捷運中山站4號出口步行5分鐘 or 中山地下街R7出口步行1分鐘）</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">營業時間<span>（週一公休）</span>：</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">平日11:00-22:00、</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">假日10:00-22:00</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">FB粉絲團&nbsp;<span style="color: #666699;"><strong><a href="https://www.facebook.com/Cocobrother.icecream/" target="_blank"><span style="color: #666699;">Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋</span></a></strong></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #008080;"><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">※不收服務費，假日低消150元</span></strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">雖然現在已經進入秋天，但還是讓我想吃冰解饞~~(給我冰!!!)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">趁著假日，我們來到「Coco Brother」，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">其實這間原先是在三和夜市擺攤賣「泰式椰子冰淇淋」，是去年才開了一間新店面，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">不只沿襲人氣招牌椰子冰淇淋，還有販售椰皇燉奶、南洋鍋物及泰式輕食!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954430"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (6).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258296-2714911025.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (6).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954424"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (5).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258291-1677228596.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (5).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">店門口的天空藍牆面，搭配椰子殼裝飾，讓人有種身在海邊的錯覺~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">「</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Life is good at the beach」</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954439"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (8).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258304-1374885991.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (8).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122958234"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (3)" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509260426-2411253052.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (3)" border="0"></a>&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954433"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (7).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258300-1132833877.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (7).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">門口的兩塊黑板分別想上營業時間及主打餐點</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954442"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (9).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258308-2592645011.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (9).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">走進店內，雖然座位空間不大，但裝潢非常溫馨，又有南洋度假的感覺~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954451"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (10).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258313-77084262.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (10).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">當初在夜市裡擺的攤販，現在已經成為店內的擺設囉</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954310"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (25).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258216-1643508723.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (25).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954307"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (24).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258211-2936556775.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (24).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">在店內可以看見許多椰子殼，畫成櫻桃小丸子的人物，真是物盡其用!!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954487"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (1).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258330-3794195576.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (1).jpg" border="0"></a>&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">Coco Brother菜單&nbsp;</span> <span style="color: #0000ff;"><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954235" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff;">請點這</span></a></span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">店內主打的椰子冰淇淋，價位約在120~130元，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">可以選擇要<span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong>椰殼裝</strong></span>或<strong><span style="color: #993300;">木碗裝</span></strong>，差別在於有沒有含椰肉</span></p>

<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954277"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (20).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258194-496030074.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (20).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">如果不想吃冰的話，也可以選擇南洋鍋物，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">目前有3種口味：新加坡叻沙鍋、檸檬香茅鍋、泰式酸辣鍋</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954319"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (26).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258221-2938382565.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (26).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">左:</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">綜合堅果養生椰</span></strong></span><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">皇燉奶 $220元</span></strong></span></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #993300;">右:椰子冰淇淋「巴那那哥哥」$120元</span></strong></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954346"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (32).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258245-3711511369.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (32).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">綜合堅果養生椰</span></strong></span><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">皇燉奶</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">一送上桌，整個椰奶香就飄香出來，味道聞起來很舒服~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">小編選的是<strong><span style="color: #993300;">綜合堅果</span></strong>口味，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">包含腰果、杏仁果和南瓜子，一旁附上蝦餅!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954340"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (30).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258237-2862421149.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (30).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">燉奶是以整顆椰子盛裝椰子水、鮮奶蛋白，三小時火侯燉煮，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">吃的時候可以把一旁的堅果加到燉奶裡，會增添不同口味唷</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954331"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (28).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258229-600574593.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (28).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">OMG燉奶滑嫩的口感，很像在吃奶酪的感覺，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但是比奶酪還要嫩，吃在口中會入口即化，暖心又暖胃~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954376"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (36).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258260-766530043.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (36).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">吃到最後，我們用鐵湯匙將椰子內側的果肉挖下來</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954394"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (38).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258268-2986578442.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (38).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">吃起來脆脆的椰子果肉，和燉奶一起拌著吃，味道也很不錯耶</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954415"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (41).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258280-3779882354.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (41).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">小豬神手把椰子內側挖得乾乾淨淨 (拍手)</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954418"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (42).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258284-1593141938.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (42).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #993300;">椰子冰淇淋「巴那那哥哥」</span></strong></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">以泰國椰子殼盛裝的椰子冰，不加任何的人工香料，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">有香蕉的清甜、巧克力濃郁的甜，配上清爽的椰子冰，</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">層次豐富不甜膩!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">沒想到椰子冰、香蕉和巧克力，三者搭在一起吃還不賴~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954364"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (35).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258256-1580912166.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (35).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">而且裡面還吃的到大塊椰子肉，沒有在騙~~有圖有真相~是真材實料啊!!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/122954388"><img title="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (37).jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1509258264-2294832310.jpg" alt="Coco Brother 椰子冰淇淋 (37).jpg" border="0"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">如果你也喜歡南洋風泰式料理，不妨逛完街就可以到Coco Brother 吃囉~</span></p>
