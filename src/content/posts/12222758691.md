<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">終於來到傳說中的台南夢幻湖～</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這裡的地形經過長期地表沖蝕及溪流侵蝕作用下，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">交錯排列成高低起伏的丘陵與溪谷，造就了景觀獨特的月世界風貌，</span></p>

<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45804044392" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4870/45804044392_6d74062d75_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區01"></a></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">門票資訊：免門票</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">開放時間：週一-週日09:30~17:00</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">牛埔村就位於台南縣龍崎鄉境內，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">經過長期累積的地表沖蝕及溪流侵蝕作用下，常寸草不生形成光禿禿的泥岩惡地，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">交錯排列於高低起伏的丘陵樣貌，也造就了月世界風貌!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45804043672" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4804/45804043672_55abb23989_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區03"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">沿路大部分都是石頭地面，走起來不會覺得太累…</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45804043332" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1919/45804043332_213562800d_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區04"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">大約幾步路後，就會出現一個休息區!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">從旁邊樓梯下去，就是木棧階梯，很多網美都在這邊拍照哦~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45804042972" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1938/45804042972_8d576ee61a_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區05"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31982078438" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1933/31982078438_e76f108a29_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區06"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">當天天氣真的很棒，完全不需要修圖~~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">最佳拍攝角度就是從上而下拍，請看下圖~~哈哈</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44940339685" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4889/44940339685_3ce7996c9c_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區07"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">樓梯下去後~就會看到斗笠涼亭，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">可以坐著稍微休息一下，不過仔細看~這涼亭並非完全遮蔽耶~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31982078058" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1915/31982078058_ae32b960a5_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區09"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45804041082" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4824/45804041082_276898d172_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區11"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">此時，沿著步道樓梯走，可以看見泥岩分布地形，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但從這角度看還不算清楚，讓我們繼續往下走吧!!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45804041332" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4859/45804041332_fe793ea348_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區10"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44940339395" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1947/44940339395_2dccc9c45b_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區12"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45804039902" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4840/45804039902_4544ef98e4_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區14"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45853196451" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4841/45853196451_45e56a6e1d_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區16"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">接著，我們將往水中涼亭的方向走，去看看台南夢幻湖!!!!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45853196211" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4878/45853196211_32277f7d15_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區17"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45853195911" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1976/45853195911_135a961efd_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區18"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">沿途會經過一個<span style="color: #008080; font-size: 14pt;">水土保持草類觀察區</span>，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">不得不誇讚一下這裡，就連各式草類名稱都標示得很清楚，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">讓人在親近大自然同時還能認識各式植物~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45804038682" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1936/45804038682_bb06f5a951_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區19"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44940353645" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4866/44940353645_33269ef984_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區20"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">走約幾分鐘後，馬上抵達水中涼亭!!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31982073418" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1946/31982073418_ccfda188a0_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區21"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31982072968" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1902/31982072968_deb6ca59b8_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區22"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這裡的視野比前半段寬廣多了~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">沒想到在一個平靜的湖面上有個地形特殊的景色!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">還能拍到湖面倒影，立刻湧入心曠神怡的心情~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31982072598" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4911/31982072598_f810b437cd_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區23"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31982072178" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4829/31982072178_eee5d396bb_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區24"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31982071848" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1938/31982071848_8c93d3bcd1_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區25"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">既然遠從台北來到此地，免不了要合影一張，哈~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31982071528" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4825/31982071528_a3ca8e4451_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區26"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31982071218" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4913/31982071218_4ce126fb70_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區27"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">當初農委會水土保持局為了水土保持並營造鄉村景觀，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">以自然工法建築讓谷地集水，作為水土保持的示範區，沒想到卻一炮而紅，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">平靜的湖面與白堊土山陵的結合，很適合來環湖步道散步!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45853194241" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4857/45853194241_537c2c8193_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區28"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31982071068" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1915/31982071068_2643f94976_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區29"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">特別的是，有一區以廢輪胎護坡法，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">利用廢輪胎來穩定邊坡，並導入植物達到資源回收再利用的目的~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45804035462" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1933/45804035462_e97e01d848_z.jpg" alt="牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區30"></a></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">「牛埔泥岩水土保持教學園區」空氣清新之外，園區也規劃的很用心，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">讓人走在步道之餘，沿路還能了解各式植物以及相關地形簡介~</span></p>
