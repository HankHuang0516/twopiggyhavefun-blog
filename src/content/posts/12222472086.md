<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">(以下為舊店面文章)</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">重新裝潢後的林東芳，店面外觀變得好高級，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">就在原本地址的旁邊巷內，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">新店面共有兩層樓的用餐空間，環境相當乾淨~</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">即使是平日中午時段來，人潮絡繹不絕!</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但別太擔心~排隊有椅子可以坐著等!</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42859745280" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1857/42859745280_ceec87b2fa_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 (14)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44669907831" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1895/44669907831_4d74933cf2_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 (15)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">牆上的紅磚頭，讓店內增添了復古風味！</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43760517595" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1878/43760517595_09eda68398_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 (16)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44669907181" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1865/44669907181_0c4d4c53ca_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 (17)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">2018台北米其林「必比登推介」名單，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">有八家牛肉麵店上榜，其中一家就是<span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #ff6600;">「林東芳牛肉麵」</span>！</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42859742480" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1878/42859742480_13f4b895f4_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 (12)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43760517045" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1892/43760517045_75597d5d7a_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 (18)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">因應店內有許多觀光客朝聖，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">所以店內的<span style="color: #ff6600; font-size: 14pt;">菜單有中英日文版</span>～</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42859742310" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1890/42859742310_c5dd436740_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 菜單 (9)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/30799142018" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1869/30799142018_196043d7c0_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 菜單 (8)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">小菜部分都是$40元</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">花干是熱騰騰送上桌，完全吸附飽滿的滷汁，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">口感就像豆包，增添了中藥滷汁香氣！</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">-</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">冰箱也有其他小菜可自取，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">我們拿小魚干、豆皮，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">其中我覺得豆皮吃起來好特別，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">有明顯煙燻的香氣，切成細條狀很好入口!</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42859742750" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1870/42859742750_5eb802a097_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 (10)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">上菜速度其實算快，大概點完餐５分鐘就送餐來～</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/30799142568" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1872/30799142568_f7907131d8_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 (3)"></a></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">半筋半肉麵 (小) $210元</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">每一塊牛肉幾乎帶筋，燉至相當軟嫩，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">牛筋呈現半透明狀態，跟湯匙差不多大小，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">軟Q又柔嫩，不會有那種軟爛的感覺…</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">而湯頭以大量牛雜燉煮20小時，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">帶點中藥香，喝起來很溫潤，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">還可以免費加湯一次唷！</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42859743430" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1879/42859743430_845161296f_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 (2)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44620448672" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1898/44620448672_bb03d5a949_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 (11)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42859742900" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1892/42859742900_d14db3b970_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 (7)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42859743100" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1893/42859743100_bd3e5ec1ba_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 (4)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這張是中午用完餐，離開時拍的照片～</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">排隊人潮比我來的時候還誇張！</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44669908311" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1876/44669908311_8ed2304665_z.jpg" alt="林東芳牛肉麵 (13)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">總結：</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">「林東芳牛肉麵」果然名不虛傳～</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">自從搬家改裝後，用餐環境比以前小吃攤好多了！</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">雖然餐點價格稍貴，小菜部分似乎也漲價，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">但是仍然美味，上菜速度也快，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">對於中午只休息一小時的上班族來說～</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">用餐不會太趕，也不用擔心下午上班遲到！</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">不過，光是半筋半肉麵小碗就要２１０元，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">我還是寧願去吃「濟南牛肉麵」啦～</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">所以再訪意願三顆星！</span></p>
