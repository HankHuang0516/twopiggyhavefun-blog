<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這裡的豬頭肉搭配油麵，吃起來有古早味的風味，裡面豬頭肉給的很多塊，咀嚼起來很軟嫩，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">然後麵條份量也是超多，而湯頭讓我蠻喜歡，可惜麵條份量多過於湯，會覺得越吃越乾的感覺(´(エ)｀)</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/46476978625" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7895/46476978625_e2c56cb2eb_z.jpg" alt="IMG_20190310_120321"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">後來才發現豬頭肉和嘴邊肉不太一樣，只是長很像啦ＸＤ</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/46476978615" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4863/46476978615_14659ffa1c_z.jpg" alt="IMG_20190310_120337"></a></span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">乾板條搭配油蔥和醬料攪拌後，整個香氣撲鼻而來，</span></p>
