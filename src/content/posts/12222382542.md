<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">「甘榜馳名海南雞飯」坐捷運很方便，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">從捷運１號出口出來後，往右後方巷子進去即可抵達。</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43325734615" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1837/43325734615_e18a850958_z.jpg" alt="IMG_0125"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43325733385" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1816/43325733385_d5b2be360b_z.jpg" alt="IMG_0126"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">「甘榜馳名海南雞飯」菜單</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #993300;">請直接點圖看放大圖</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">主要販售正宗馬來西亞海南雞飯，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">可以搭配套餐之外，也可單點海南雞飯便當，</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/30364032018" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1874/30364032018_ae5e3e6d6e_z.jpg" alt="IMG_0127"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">內用還有<span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #339966;">沙勞越胡椒湯</span>可免費續！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">沙勞越胡椒湯是以純正馬來西亞胡椒製作，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">入口後～胡椒的嗆味蠻重，但卻相當開胃～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/30364030668" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1887/30364030668_41b6a810e2_z.jpg" alt="IMG_0128"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">座位區共有２間，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">裝潢很有馬來西亞的風格，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">繽紛的牆面，讓人看的心情很愉悅～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44231619741" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1889/44231619741_e4640ca369_z.jpg" alt="IMG_0129"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44182745962" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1890/44182745962_18811aff1a_z.jpg" alt="IMG_0130"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">B餐_海南雞飯+飲品2選1 $160元</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">去骨雞腿肉鋪上辣椒醬、青辣椒醬、薑末醬，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">一旁還有青蔥、豆芽菜及黃瓜片可解膩</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44182744482" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1900/44182744482_55ce634300.jpg" alt="IMG_0131"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">必須稱讚一下這裡的海南雞，是每日現場現煮雞腿肉，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">外皮油油亮亮，每口咬下肉質超鮮嫩~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44182738682" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1813/44182738682_c98ffb2eba.jpg" alt="IMG_0136"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">米飯部分是使用泰國米，粒粒分明，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">而且吸收了雞汁，吃起來不會太乾硬～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29296044547" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1852/29296044547_b5ea8beeaf.jpg" alt="IMG_0137"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">套餐搭配的「大馬竹蔗水」</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">以精選天然食材竹蔗、馬蹄和白茅根製成</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">口味帶甜（微糖）、清爽解渴~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44182737722" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1864/44182737722_7143fbeef2_z.jpg" alt="IMG_0138"></a>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">總結：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">平常在台灣很少看到賣正宗馬來西亞海南雞飯，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">第一次吃個人覺得口味相當道地～</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">尤其是海南雞腿肉，每一口都很鮮嫩，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">搭配五彩醬料：黃醬、紅醬、綠醬、黑醬、秘製醬。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">讓雞腿肉吃起來不會太單調，反而多了另一種層次！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">來到捷運中山站，不知道吃什麼的話，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">這家「甘榜馳名海南雞飯」給大家參考囉！</span></p>
