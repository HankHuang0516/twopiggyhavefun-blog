<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><img title="2015-12-11_21.40.39" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1450066704-786770553.jpg" alt="2015-12-11_21.40.39" border="0"> &nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 新細明體, serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">這幾天的低溫，讓人冷得實在「凍未條」<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/whatchutalkingabout_smile.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">這時候最適合來碗熱呼呼的～<strong><span style="color: #ff0000;">黑糖薑汁地瓜湯</span></strong>!!<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/heart.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">冬天喝薑湯具有驅寒的效果，加上富含膳食纖維的地瓜，是一道可飽足又營養的甜湯!</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">一起來看看要怎麼煮吧~</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #0000ff;"><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; text-decoration: underline;">準備食材：</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">地瓜(去皮)約700g、老薑ㄧ塊適量、黑糖60g、台糖金砂糖40g、鹽少許、水約1200ml</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #0000ff;"><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; text-decoration: underline;">做法<span style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">：</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">1.&nbsp;地瓜洗淨去皮，切成適當大小塊。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px;">2.&nbsp;鍋中放入水，再加入</span><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px;">拍扁的老薑片煮開，讓薑香充分地釋放出來。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">3. 接著，放入切好的地瓜，煮滾後倒入黑糖和金砂糖，以中小火煮至地瓜熟軟，約10分鐘。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">4.&nbsp;起鍋前加入少許鹽調味，可以解甜膩，湯頭也會更甘美。</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">只要10分鐘，黑糖薑汁地瓜湯就完成嚕!!</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: 新細明體, serif; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal;">口感綿密又鬆軟的地瓜，充滿黑糖香氣</span><span style="font-size: 11pt;" lang="EN-US">!</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: 新細明體, serif; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal;">讓人口口意猶未盡</span><span style="font-size: 11pt;" lang="EN-US">~~</span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: 新細明體, serif; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal;">絕對是冬天最棒甜品<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/heart.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151211_212040_941" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1450067892-2630191409.jpg" alt="IMG_20151211_212040_941" border="0"> &nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">薑的好處多多，可以舒緩經痛、預防感冒、促進消化、改善血液循環……</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #000000;">在煮薑汁地瓜湯時，先煮老薑釋放味道，再與地瓜、黑糖一同烹煮，對於女性改善經痛問題非常有效哦！</span><br></span></p>
