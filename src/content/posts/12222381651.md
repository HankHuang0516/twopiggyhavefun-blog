<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">說到捷運中山站周邊的美食～</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">我個人喜歡吃的一家店是「福大蒸餃館」，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">每次來都必點一籠蒸餃、酸辣湯，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">一餐不用２００元就能飽餐一頓！</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">每次用餐時間去生意好到爆炸的「福大蒸餃館」</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">店內只有一位阿姨負責點餐，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">我看每桌都一定會點”蒸餃”，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">只是蒸餃不會那麼快上，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">至少等約１０～１５分鐘～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/30341552988" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1847/30341552988_a37c51ac5c.jpg" alt="福大蒸餃館 (1)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">店門口有直接放上菜單，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">可以先評估一下，再決定是否進去！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43491290904" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1849/43491290904_8a9cc7e915.jpg" alt="福大蒸餃館 (13)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">「福大蒸餃館」菜單</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #0000ff;">請直接點圖看放大圖喔～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43491290704" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1897/43491290704_dde07e0c46.jpg" alt="福大蒸餃館 菜單 (2)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">蒸餃 $90元</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">蒸餃一籠共有10顆，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">每顆餡料飽滿又大顆，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">比外面賣的水餃還大顆～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43491293274" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1857/43491293274_ab73866069.jpg" alt="福大蒸餃館 (4)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">你看～剛好放滿湯匙的大小！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43303348305" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1858/43303348305_e52ec410be.jpg" alt="福大蒸餃館 (5)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">重點是~咬下一口，會有湯汁流出來！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這時候就是要連同餡料一起吃下去才美味～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43303347855" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1833/43303347855_e86ff848e0.jpg" alt="福大蒸餃館 (7)"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43491292154" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1883/43491292154_0d0e66b82c.jpg" alt="福大蒸餃館 (8)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">吃完這一籠～真的覺得肚子快炸開，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">可能是我當天還沒有很餓吧～哈</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/30341551048" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1864/30341551048_be12ea79e0.jpg" alt="福大蒸餃館 (9)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">牛蛋湯 $60元</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">每次來都是點這裡的酸辣湯，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這次試試看一般外面少見的「牛蛋湯」</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43303347435" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1898/43303347435_9016be3a16.jpg" alt="福大蒸餃館 (10)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">第一次在外頭喝到牛蛋湯，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">其實就是牛肉清湯加上蛋花，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 14pt;">口味上稍辣</span>，辣度是嘴裡有點麻麻的感覺，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但喝到最後會覺得很油，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">可以整碗喝完我佩服他！</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><br></span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43491291104" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1857/43491291104_ec0dddefae.jpg" alt="福大蒸餃館 (11)"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/30341550758" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1814/30341550758_9c4bbcabb8.jpg" alt="福大蒸餃館 (12)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">總結：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">美食總是需要時間等待的！</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #008080;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">我個人真的很喜歡吃會噴汁</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">的蒸餃，</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">每一顆餡料飽滿又會噴汁，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">就湯類而言，我還是比較喜歡喝「酸辣湯」</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">下次有機會再跟大家分享照片～</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">－</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #008080;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">當天一個人吃一籠蒸餃、牛蛋湯，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">真的是飽到爆炸！</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">唯一的缺點是～店內手機訊號很差，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">手機已經是吃到飽，但網路卻跑不動= =</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">也許這是翻桌率快的原因吧XD</span></p>
