<p><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: inherit; font-size: 12pt; vertical-align: baseline;">這份是老闆招待的，滷的很入味</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">是非常開胃的海鮮冷盤，非常新鮮珍貴的食材</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">有鮑魚沙拉、日式蟹肉棒、涼拌花枝，份量都十人份剛剛好~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 微軟正黑體; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; color: #ff6600;">🔶第三道:&nbsp;</span></span>白斬雞</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">整隻土雞下去切的，涼涼的雞腿肉與雞胸肉吃起來可以彈牙的那種</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">咬下去可以嘗到雞汁的香味充滿口腔，尤其雞腿肉特別鮮嫩~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">整體煮的恰到好處，不像很多合菜的白斬雞會有明顯的血色</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">份量也算很多，CP值 up up ~💖</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">桂竹筍燉的蠻入味，脆脆的在嘴裡卡滋卡滋~</span></p>

<p><span style="font-family: 微軟正黑體; font-size: 12pt;">重點是麵線味道很濃郁，搭配油蔥提味，又油油亮亮</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 微軟正黑體; font-size: 12pt;">吸吸簌簌一口接一口好滑順，光麵線我可以吃一整盤!<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/tounge_smile.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">除了滿滿的豬肉、筍絲、鳥蛋，湯頭的部分用燉芋頭來增加沙沙的口感</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">可見山豬城的廚師處理的很好，<span style="color: #ff9900;">皮不焦、不見血、肉彈牙</span>，這三個關鍵都掌握到了</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">完全可以充當山豬城招牌名菜，大幅拉高CP值!&nbsp;💖💖💖</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">用雞熬成的精華賦予藥膳湯生命，雖然雞肉因熬煮悶燉會稍老，不過湯頭卻能讓人暖胃!</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">有一個疑問一直埋藏在我心中，有很多合菜常常都會把季節時蔬放在最後一道菜</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">可是大家都吃得很飽，才上這道配飯的菜餚，實在是無法理解~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">而這次是炒高麗菜，炒的也入味，不過就沒特別留影</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-weight: inherit; color: #ff6600; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 微軟正黑體; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="font-weight: inherit; color: #ff6600; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 微軟正黑體; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><a style="font-family: 微軟正黑體;" href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/139864800"><br></a><span style="color: #ff6600; font-family: 微軟正黑體; font-weight: inherit; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">🔶第十三道:四季水果-西瓜</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">水果的部分都是比較常見的，沒特別留影</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
