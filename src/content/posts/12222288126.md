<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">上次爬完「<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #ff6600; text-decoration: underline;"><a href="http://t.cn/RDz1n04" target="_blank"><span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #ff6600; text-decoration: underline;">鶯歌石登山步道</span></a></span></span>」，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">傍晚我們來吃這家「勇伯垃圾麵」</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">店門口外觀寫著大大的招牌很好認～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29950383918" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/862/29950383918_2b1a1a79f7_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9278"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這裡是沒有菜單，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">所以點餐時直接告知服務人員!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43103298694" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1819/43103298694_b7e42b7c8b_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9279"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">我覺得店內座位有些髒亂，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但通常這種小吃店好像都這樣～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43103297604" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1832/43103297604_19d614207a_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9284"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">招牌垃圾麵是湯麵<span style="color: #ff0000;">(微辣)</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">不吃辣請提前告知喔！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">我們各點一碗辣、不辣的垃圾麵，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">麵條有分成粗、細！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42011403340" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1798/42011403340_70af251275_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9286"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">辣的這碗是粗麵條，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">湯頭有些微辣開胃~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">裡頭還加了幾塊紅燒肉、豬頭肉!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29950381438" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1796/29950381438_032403ae61_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9287"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42011403020" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1811/42011403020_3716be2d84_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9288"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">再來，我這碗是<span style="color: #ff0000;">不辣</span>的湯麵～</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">口味上吃起來就是傳統的湯麵，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">豬大骨熬製的湯頭喝起來很清爽！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42011402710" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/849/42011402710_86fd7e4e45_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9290"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">小菜有分一人份、兩人份，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">圖片中是一人份量唷～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29950382828" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/941/29950382828_7aab8279dd_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9280"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">紅燒肉外皮炸得很香酥，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">沾上一旁的醬汁很不錯!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43103298054" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/928/43103298054_0c122b2337_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9281"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">油豆腐大約是兩大塊，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">口感軟嫩，口味中規中矩~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但醬油膏偏多，吃到後面覺得有點鹹!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29950382338" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/861/29950382338_d981fbf79b_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9282"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">大腸送上桌時，已經淋上醬油膏!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這道大腸我覺得蠻好吃，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">不會有那種很生的味道~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29950380768" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1837/29950380768_e9e8d030f0_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9283"><br></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">來說一下～垃圾麵的由來：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">早期鶯歌為陶瓷重鎮，因需要大量工人，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">第一代是推著小攤車四處叫賣維生，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">而麵的湯頭是以大量豬骨熬製，從不去渣，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">所以湯頭色澤也略顯混濁，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">並加上特製肉燥豬油、紅燒肉渣及紅燒肉、豬頸肉、韭菜，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">也因為湯頭有些渣渣像ㄌㄚˋ　ㄙㄚˋ　ㄚˋ，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">雖然賣相不佳～但卻美味可口，因此被笑稱ㄌㄚˋ　ㄙㄚˋ麵</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43103296554" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1831/43103296554_5ca3eca2cd_z.jpg" alt="IMG_9289"></a></p>
