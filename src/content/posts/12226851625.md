<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53340132189" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53340132189_7580b756bb_c.jpg" alt=""></a></span></p>

<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53340133474" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53340133474_83fe4b6cfc_c.jpg" alt=""></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這家美術館是由台灣畫家吳冠德所創立，除了展示多種藝術畫作，還有許多懷舊的老物件。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53340133234" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53340133234_5a69f86786_c.jpg" alt=""></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這區則是綠意盎然的天井，有著陽光灑落屋內，嚷室內空間格外顯得清新自然。</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53339792916" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53339792916_000598724c_c.jpg" alt=""></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53340254100" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53340254100_4ce4823624_c.jpg" alt=""></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53339801196" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53339801196_f24a49e507_c.jpg" alt=""></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">中間的藝術展區空間，一整面白牆搭配各種畫作，雖然不太懂作者想表達的理念，但我相信觀賞這些畫作的人會因各自不同的心境而有不同的體驗。</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53340014113" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53340014113_7657afac2e_c.jpg" alt="新北三峽｜庶民美術館"></a></p>
