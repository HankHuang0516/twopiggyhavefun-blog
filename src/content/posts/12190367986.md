<p><span style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #0000ff;">** 喜歡旅遊、愛吃美食嗎？歡迎加入&nbsp;</span><span style="color: #777777; font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; text-decoration: underline;"><a style="color: #000000; text-decoration: underline;" href="https://www.facebook.com/twopiggyhavefun/?fref=ts" target="_blank"><span style="color: #ff0000; text-decoration: underline;">兩隻小豬</span><span style="color: #ff0000; text-decoration: underline;">粉絲團</span></a></span><span style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #0000ff;">**</span></p>
<p><img style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;" title="page" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1455682829-719286986.jpg" alt="page" border="0"><span style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">這一鍋，集結千年歷史，取自武則天時期祕傳的「榮華富貴」鍋及宜妃最愛「滋補養生」的概念，加入各類現代新鮮食材，將皇室祕藏千年、滋補名菜的「鳳凰回巢」鍋重現於這一鍋。</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">用餐這天被安排到2樓座位，趁客人還不多時，來拍張走道~</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">店家裝潢充滿古代朝廷風格</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160209_213132_089" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1455682882-1063320805.jpg" alt="IMG_20160209_213132_089" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">&nbsp;一到2樓，可以看到兵馬俑英勇的姿勢</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160209_213142_077" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1455682882-1457320065.jpg" alt="IMG_20160209_213142_077" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff0000;">消費方式&gt;&gt;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">基本上一種鍋底就是$110／每人</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">加一種鍋底是$150／每桌</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000;">有一區是白飯跟醬料，白飯可以無限吃！</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000;">店家還會提供圍兜兜，以免吃的太開心沾到衣服</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><img style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;" title="IMG_20160209_213056_966" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1455682882-3880195339.jpg" alt="IMG_20160209_213056_966" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">單點肉品價格大約$220~$330元</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">(左)<strong>梅花豬肉</strong>，(中)<strong>爆漿魚子球</strong>，(右)<strong>綜合魚丸</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">個人覺得爆獎起司丸真的超讚，我完全是個裡面有包起司的食物都愛~哈哈</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160209_213148_459" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1455682882-3369965247.jpg" alt="IMG_20160209_213148_459" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">忘記這叫什麼肉片..</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">擺盤的很精緻，肉片也很大片，肉質新鮮好吃，雖然帶點油脂，但不會太油膩</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160209_213239_032" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1455682882-3551268253.jpg" alt="IMG_20160209_213239_032" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff0000;"><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">藥膳湯+酸菜鍋</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">這兩種湯頭都好好喝，尤其是酸菜湯，裡面還有放幾片豬肉，最後喝不完還有打包回家XD</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160209_213154_437" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1455682882-2260738810.jpg" alt="IMG_20160209_213154_437" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160209_213200_468" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1455682882-1505749519.jpg" alt="IMG_20160209_213200_468" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">用餐到最後會送上蔓越莓試管冰沙、糖葫蘆，是可以再續的唷!試管冰沙酸酸甜甜的，剛好可以解油膩~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160209_213317_978" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1455682882-111873877.jpg" alt="IMG_20160209_213317_978" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">整體來說是很棒的用餐經驗，單點食材算是在中價位，還好有白飯、冰沙可以無限取用，就不用擔心會吃不飽XD</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">用餐環境氣氛也不錯，服務人員態度良好，推薦大家如果要慶祝節日或是家庭聚餐，可以來這一鍋用餐也不錯唷~</span></p>
