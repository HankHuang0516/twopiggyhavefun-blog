<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">當天兩隻小豬是吃<span style="color: #008000;"><strong>精選兩人套餐 $980元</strong></span></span></span></p>

<p><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 新細明體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #777777; letter-spacing: 1px;"><img title="IMAG0015" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353114-3236768323.jpg" alt="IMAG0015" border="0"></span></p>

<p><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 新細明體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #777777; letter-spacing: 1px;"><img title="IMAG0017" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353114-3535574838.jpg" alt="IMAG0017" border="0"></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這裡的<span style="color: #ff0000;">韓式小菜是無限續</span>喔~~還會定期更換菜色</span></p>
<p><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 新細明體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #777777; letter-spacing: 1px;"><img title="IMAG0018" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353114-2126695324.jpg" alt="IMAG0018" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 新細明體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #777777; letter-spacing: 1px;"><img title="IMAG0019" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353114-2899863890.jpg" alt="IMAG0019" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 新細明體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #777777; letter-spacing: 1px;"><img title="IMAG0021" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353114-1148062402.jpg" alt="IMAG0021" border="0"> &nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 新細明體;"><span style="font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px;">兩人套餐可以選兩種飲料</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: 新細明體;"><span style="font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px;">我們選了兩種不同口味的</span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">韓國葡萄汁&amp;水梨汁</span></p>
<p><img title="IMAG0022" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353559-297944974.jpg" alt="IMAG0022" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #800080;">韓國葡萄汁</span>可以喝到果粒口感，搭配烤肉超適合</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #ff9900;">韓國水梨汁</span>味道偏甜，但剛好可以減緩嘴巴裡的辣味</span></p>
<p><img title="IMAG0042" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353559-3401523243.jpg" alt="IMAG0042" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #ff0000;">所有的套餐均含小菜無限供應</span>，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">有</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span>雜菜、<span>白蘿蔔、<span>泡菜、<span>小魚豆干、<span>花椰菜</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span><span><span><span><span><span style="font-size: 16px;">雜菜、</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">白蘿蔔、<span style="font-size: 16px;">小魚豆干我們續了好幾回，</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>越吃越唰嘴XD</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p><img title="IMAG0025" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353559-3824257997.jpg" alt="IMAG0025" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">生菜盤非常豐富，有韓國生菜、紅葉生菜、羅曼生菜、蒜仁、綠辣椒片，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff0000;">加點需要$100元</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">左邊的拌蔥絲看起來很辣，我們幾乎原封不動...</span></p>
<p><img title="IMAG0029" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353559-1118501311.jpg" alt="IMAG0029" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">青翠色澤的生菜盤，好期待趕快夾肉一起吃~~</span></p>
<p><img title="IMAG0028" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353559-1836336317.jpg" alt="IMAG0028" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">醬料總共有三種，<span style="color: #993300;">胡椒鹽</span>、<span style="color: #808000;">胡椒鹽加韓國麻油</span>、<span style="color: #ff0000;">韓式辣醬</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">其中以胡椒鹽加韓國麻油最讓我喜歡，最能襯托出肉的美味</span></span></p>
<p><img title="IMAG0024" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353559-2080715898.jpg" alt="IMAG0024" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">精選兩人套餐的肉有三種，分別是松阪豬、韓國水梨醃豬五花、豬五花</span></p>
<p><img title="IMAG0027" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353559-2054572065.jpg" alt="IMAG0027" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">在新沙洞韓國烤肉，最享受的地方就是有<span style="font-size: 16px;">桌邊服務!!</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">有專人會烤五花肉給你吃，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">不用自己烤那麼辛苦</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">就有更多時間和好姊妹聊天~</span></p>
<p><img title="IMAG0030" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353559-2936403902.jpg" alt="IMAG0030" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">看著肉在烤盤上滋滋作響的聲音，讓人想大開殺戒阿~~</span></p>
<p><img title="IMAG0031" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353559-646603800.jpg" alt="IMAG0031" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">在我們續了好幾回的小菜後，服務人員也將五花肉烤好囉~~</span></p>
<p><img title="IMAG0037" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353559-2403798601.jpg" alt="IMAG0037" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">服務人員會將肉剪成適當大小，再放在盤子中</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">豬五花口感紮實，用生菜包著吃真的不錯，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但油脂比其他部位較肥滋滋，吃多會有些膩口</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">豬松坂比較沒那麼多油脂，吃起來脆脆口感</span></p>
<p><img title="IMAG0038" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353559-1865221262.jpg" alt="IMAG0038" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">烤好的五花肉剪成小條狀比較容易包在生菜中，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">選擇想沾的醬料再加上韓式泡菜，剛好綜合掉五花肉的油膩感</span></p>
<p><img title="IMAG0041" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353559-3427995124.jpg" alt="IMAG0041" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">海鮮豆腐鍋(附白飯)</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">充滿濃郁韓式風味的<strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #ff6600;">海鮮豆腐鍋</span>，</span></strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">湯頭有些微辣</span></span></p>
<p><img title="IMAG0032" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353559-985334920.jpg" alt="IMAG0032" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">裡面的配料相當豐富，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">都是泡菜、豆腐、蛋、豬肉片、金針菇，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">還有蛤蠣和蝦子，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">豆腐軟嫩到入口即化，配上熱呼呼的湯頭，超級開胃!</span></p>
<p><img title="IMAG0036" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481353559-3409054911.jpg" alt="IMAG0036" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">舒適的用餐空間，還有專人烤肉的感覺很好，韓式小菜想吃都可以再續~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">烤得香噴噴的五花肉加上一點泡菜，最後包著生菜一口吃下，完全中和了肉的油膩感</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">是個適合好友聚餐的地方，推薦給喜歡韓式烤肉的朋友們</span></p>
