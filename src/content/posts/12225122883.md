<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">是一台<strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #ff6600; text-decoration: underline;">六面多功能手拍鼓</span></span></strong>，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">一個玩具就結合了六大功能，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">包含：<span style="background-color: #ffcc99; color: #ffffff;">鋼琴按鍵</span>/<span style="background-color: #99cc00; color: #ffffff;">模擬電話</span>/<span style="background-color: #ff99cc; color: #ffffff;">圖形配對</span>/<span style="background-color: #cc99ff; color: #ffffff;">趣味串珠</span>/<span style="background-color: #33cccc; color: #ffffff;">轉動齒輪</span>/<span style="background-color: #c0c0c0; color: #ffffff;">手拍鼓面</span>，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">不僅讓小朋友動手動腦，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">當作親子互動的遊戲也不錯，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">詳細的功能介紹一起往下看吧～～～</span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">感覺六面可以玩很久，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">剛好也有在做特價＄５９９就給它買下啦～</span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;<span style="font-size: 12pt;">裡面的積木和電話是另外包裝，</span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">每個功能和模式都寫得清清楚楚～</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/50599083422" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/50599083422_a30c4381e1_c.jpg" alt="IMG_20201113_141203"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 微軟正黑體; font-size: 18.6667px; text-align: center;">▲<span style="background-color: #ffff99;">鋼琴按鍵</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;<span style="font-size: 12pt;">這面是<span style="background-color: #ffcc00; color: #ffffff;">小狗按鍵</span>、<span style="background-color: #99cc00; color: #ffffff;">模式切換鍵</span>、<span style="background-color: #cc99ff; color: #ffffff;">安眠音樂</span>和<span style="background-color: #ff99cc; color: #ffffff;">鋼琴按鍵</span>，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px;"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #ff6600; text-decoration: underline;">短按小狗按鍵</span></span></strong>就會撥放狗叫聲及一只哈巴狗的歌曲；</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px;"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #ff6600; text-decoration: underline;">模式切換鍵</span></span></strong>共有３種模式，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">分別是音階模式、動物模式、常規模式；</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">而<strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #ff6600; text-decoration: underline;">安眠音樂</span></span></strong>共有９３２首，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">像是：莫扎特、平安夜、抓泥鰍等等、</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">再來<strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #ff6600; text-decoration: underline;">鋼琴按鍵</span></span></strong>也非常豐富有趣，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">在<strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #99cc00; text-decoration: underline;">音階模式</span></span></strong>下，短按對應的按鍵會有ＤＯ、ＲＥ、ＭＩ、ＦＡ那些聲音，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">而<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><strong><span style="color: #99cc00; text-decoration: underline;">動物模式</span></strong></span>就會撥放小鳥、猴子、小雞和老虎聲音，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">最後，<strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #99cc00; text-decoration: underline;">常規模式</span></span></strong>則是撥放按鍵上對應的內容！</span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;<span style="font-size: 12pt;">打開狗門還會有提示語音，</span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: 微軟正黑體; font-size: 18.6667px; text-align: center;">▲<span style="background-color: #ffff99;">趣味串珠</span></span></p>
