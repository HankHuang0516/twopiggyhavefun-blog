<div class="pixnet-article prose max-w-none">
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">小豬從以前到現在就很喜歡喝黑麥汁，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">不論是即開即飲、或是玻璃瓶裝的黑麥汁，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">只要逛賣場看到，就會順手帶幾瓶～</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">而黑麥汁除了單喝之外，其實還可以有很多變化唷</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">今天就來教大家如何調出夏日飲品！</span></p>
<p><img title="IMG_8032" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1531276657-3061780411.jpg" alt="IMG_8032" border="0"> &nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">台酒可以說是台灣歷史悠久的發酵技術專家，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">自成立以來成功研發出台酒特有的紅麴、紅酒萃取精華、酒粕萃取精華、</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">啤酒酵母與異麥芽寡醣等核心原料，並陸續開發出多項產品….</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">最讓人印象深刻的就是他們家的<strong>紅麴養生薄餅</strong>、<strong>花雕雞泡麵</strong>～</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 14pt;">台酒黑麥汁</span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">共有四種口味：<span style="background-color: #ffff99;">原味（減糖25%）</span>、<span style="color: #000000; background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="background-color: #ff99cc;">紅麴</span>、<span style="background-color: #ffcc99;">烏梅</span>及<span style="background-color: #ccffcc;">薑汁</span>口味</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這四種口味都是非酒精性飲料，</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 14pt;">無添加焦糖色素、無香料，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">素食者也可飲用的天然健康飲品！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">提醒大家～<span style="color: #ff6600;">開封後請冷藏</span>，並儘速飲用完畢，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">冰涼飲用，感受更香醇唷～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41522843110" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1761/41522843110_7af9bfc38c_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (2)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">為了要調出夏日飲品，小豬特別去超市選了幾項果汁，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">包含：蜜桃Ｃ、草莓檸檬汁、椰奶、葡萄果汁、蔓越莓汁</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41522842370" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/918/41522842370_d0335a6a85_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (3)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong><span style="color: #ff0000;">黑麥蜜桃鮮果茶</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">食材：紅麴黑麥汁、蜜桃Ｃ、水果切丁、檸檬片</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41522836060" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1805/41522836060_1af0373e38_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (9)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">首先，將紅麴黑麥汁倒入玻璃杯約２５０ＣＣ</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 14pt;">紅麴黑麥汁</span>裡頭添加台酒特有紅麴與淡色、濃色全穀大麥芽、</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">啤酒花萃取物等進行發酵所製成。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">麥汁的釀造過程與啤酒製程相同，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但麥汁在釀造過程中產生酒的成份前就停止發酵，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">因此麥汁是<span style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 14pt;">不含酒精</span>喔！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41522833450" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/923/41522833450_07dedb1df4_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (11)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">再倒入蜜桃Ｃ約７０ＣＣ</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41522832100" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1803/41522832100_00baed10b7_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (12)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">然後加入水果放置八分滿</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">水果我們準備的是：芒果、西瓜、鳳梨</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">可以依照自己喜歡的水果做變化唷～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43282420752" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1782/43282420752_7db13e8d5c_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (13)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">用攪拌棒攪拌均勻～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43282420202" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1782/43282420202_804b75b8dd_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (14)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">杯沿以檸檬片裝飾再加點冰磚就完成啦！！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41522830470" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/837/41522830470_17ca259f93_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (15)"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41522830070" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/846/41522830070_231709f8de_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (16)"></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">再來，要示範的是<span style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 14pt;">黑麥葡萄莓果茶</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">食材：<span style="font-size: 16px;">原味</span>黑麥汁、葡萄果汁、蔓越莓汁</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">首先，將原味黑麥汁倒入玻璃杯約２５０ＣＣ</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">原味黑麥汁本身甜度比紅麴口味<span style="color: #ff6600; font-size: 14pt;">減糖25%</span>，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">以精選麥芽以德式工藝發酵釀製而成，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">單喝麥汁相當濃郁、香氣十足～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41522827530" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/834/41522827530_97402d291a_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (19)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">再倒入蔓越莓汁約５０ＣＣ</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41522826070" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/921/41522826070_19b3982c64_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (21)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">接續倒入葡萄果汁約５０ＣＣ</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41522823910" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/846/41522823910_d5b1ff9234_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (24)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">然後加入水果</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43282416262" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1767/43282416262_228f118740_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (25)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">用攪拌棒攪拌均勻～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41522822250" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1821/41522822250_b79a581b05_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (26)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">杯沿</span>以檸檬片裝飾再加點冰磚就完成啦！！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41522821420" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1810/41522821420_b2b904d1b3_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (27)"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43282414522" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1769/43282414522_69a4467480_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (28)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">燈燈燈燈～</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">左邊是～<span style="background-color: #ff99cc; font-size: 14pt;">黑麥蜜桃鮮果茶</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">右邊那杯是～<span style="background-color: #cc99ff; font-size: 14pt;">黑麥葡萄莓果茶</span></span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41522820090" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1762/41522820090_cebea1a96a_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (29)"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43282413262" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1805/43282413262_7792d5bdc8_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (30)"></a></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600; font-size: 14pt;">黑麥蜜桃鮮果茶</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">使用1:1的紅麴黑麥汁與蜜桃C果汁調配～</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">顏色變得超美～有一種漸層的ＦＵ！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">紅麴口味的黑麥汁本身帶微微的啤酒麥芽香當基底，喝起來微氣泡口感，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">搭配水蜜桃果汁酸甜來增添水果茶的口味，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">兩者搭配非常和諧與順口，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">真的很像躺在沙灘上邊曬日光浴邊享受水果茶的那種悠哉！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43282412512" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm1.staticflickr.com/839/43282412512_360f2c8640_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (31)"></a></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600; font-size: 14pt;">黑麥葡萄莓果茶</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">使用2:1:1的原味減糖黑麥汁搭配葡萄與蔓越莓原汁！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">原味的黑麥汁較有濃醇的麥香，甜度相對較低，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">所以會搭配葡萄與蔓越莓原汁，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">原汁更能夠帶出莓果茶先酸後甜的滋味並衝擊著氣泡，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">最後尾勁來個濃厚的麥香味，一口喝下層次豐富又爽快！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43282411562" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1801/43282411562_1c6037d66f_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (32)"></a></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600; font-size: 14pt;">麥香微泡椰奶</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">使用2:1的原味減糖黑麥汁搭配進口的椰奶飲品，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">沒想到椰奶搭上原味黑麥汁，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">能夠支撐麥芽香的香味餘留時間，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">而且清爽不膩口，總體讓麥香味更加濃郁，香味更持久。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41522841310" target="_blank"><img title="" src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1786/41522841310_bdc57976e8_z.jpg" alt="台酒生技黑麥汁 (4)"></a>&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><img title="IMG_7993" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1531284813-1265115755.jpg" alt="IMG_7993" border="0">&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">不論是喝哪種口味，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">夏天飲用時添加冰塊，爽口消暑！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">冬天飲用時可隔水加熱著喝～</span></p>
