<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">今天要跟大家介紹<strong><span style="background-color: #ffff99; color: #ff6600;">LOBOR Watches</span></strong>的手錶，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這款名稱是<strong><span style="color: #993300;">New York Bethune Brown</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">外觀設計看起來簡單又大方，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">外包裝是用很有質感的盒子裝起來，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">並附上產品說明書和小卡。</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42728996021" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1757/42728996021_f8e7c09bd9_z.jpg" alt="LOBOR手錶 (10)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">手錶的包裝設計也別有用心，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">裡頭將手錶用個白線固定住，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">避免手錶放在盒子裡在運送過程中撞傷～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/27860023897" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1730/27860023897_0f2961d5a8_z.jpg" alt="LOBOR手錶 (12)"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42728995641" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1740/42728995641_3aa4d23254_z.jpg" alt="LOBOR手錶 (11)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">簡單來介紹一下LOBOR這個品牌～</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #0000ff;">LOBOR 成立於1989年，以精湛的製錶工藝逾30年的歷史聞名於世。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #0000ff;">並於瑞士及歐盟註冊品牌，深獲全球各地鐘錶愛好者和鑑賞家的推崇。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;">(文字取自官網)</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42728997701" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1726/42728997701_2a5e761885_z.jpg" alt="LOBOR手錶 (2)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="background-color: #ffff99; color: #0000ff;">LOBOR</span> 這個名字，是由創辦人兼資深鐘匠他的姓氏，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">配上母親的名字組合而成，象徵著過去的珍貴傳家寶。</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42728997521" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1736/42728997521_570e50c724_z.jpg" alt="LOBOR手錶 (3)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">LOBOR腕錶宗旨是「<span style="background-color: #ffff99; color: #993300;">For Those Who Value Time —獻給那些珍視時間的人</span>」，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">每一枚手錶都象徵著傳統的製錶技藝</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">和經久不衰的優雅，讓奢華變得觸手可及。</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42728997221" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1760/42728997221_31e0666e51_z.jpg" alt="LOBOR手錶 (4)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">LOBOR的手錶主要分為石英和機械款式，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">機芯都是採用日本機芯，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">款式時尚多變、奢華大氣！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/27860020587" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1740/27860020587_3959d56827_z.jpg" alt="LOBOR手錶 (14)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">⌚️<strong><span style="color: #993300;">New York Bethune Brown</span></strong>&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">錶帶是義大利皮革材質，摸起來很有質感～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42728995511" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1742/42728995511_df144d5743_z.jpg" alt="LOBOR手錶 (15)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">白色錶盤加上玫瑰金錶殼，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">整體給人一種低調奢華的樣子！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">而玫瑰金錶殼是以拋光不鏽鋼技術呈現，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">每個角度看都會閃閃發光✨✨</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42728994451" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1723/42728994451_294d1a2cb9_z.jpg" alt="LOBOR手錶 (16)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">日本機芯精緻腕錶，有基本的防水性！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">一般來說，手錶防水有分成四大等級：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">像這款手錶可以<span style="background-color: #ffff99;">防水30米</span>，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">比較適用於被雨水濺到或是單純洗手...</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">即使水濺到手錶表面，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">也不會影響手錶運作喔！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/27860021637" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1740/27860021637_c61179b0d2_z.jpg" alt="LOBOR手錶 (22)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這款New York Bethune Brown</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">我挑選的是40mm的大錶面~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">搭配一旁的玫瑰金屬框更能展現時尚奢華的設計～</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42728996401" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1738/42728996401_c96657de77_z.jpg" alt="LOBOR手錶 (9)"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">錶盤上面還有顯示日期的功能，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這樣就知道是幾號啦😍</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">適合郊遊及日常生活配戴～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/42728997861" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1755/42728997861_ebf98a48e4_z.jpg" alt="LOBOR手錶 (1)"></a>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">也很適合常常跑業務的朋友，更能顯得專業</span></p>
