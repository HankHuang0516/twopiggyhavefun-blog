<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;<span style="color: #008080;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">最近冷颼颼的天氣，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">最適合來碗<span style="background-color: #ffff99;">麻油雞</span>暖心暖胃😝</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">今天介紹一家<span style="color: #0000ff;">捷運江子翠2號出口</span>附近的麻油雞攤販，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">麻油雞配料可以自己任選要加什麼，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #008080;">像是：雞腿、雞塊、鳳翅或是百菇，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #008080;">而<span style="color: #ff9900;">綜合裡頭可以一次吃到所有配料</span>，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #99cc00;">湯頭也越喝越順口，不會太油膩～</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;<span style="color: #008080; font-family: 微軟正黑體; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; text-align: center;">詳細的地理位置、菜單價位和餐點表現，一起往下滑看看吧😍</span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ffffff; background-color: #cc99ff;">【江子翠麻油雞攤販】</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #993366;">地址：新北市板橋區文化路二段319號（金磚烤鴨前）</span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">前陣子經過板橋金磚烤鴨店時，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><br></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">發現前面有個麻油雞攤販，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">不知是不是因為冬天關係才開，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">因為之前都沒有看過😌</span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;<span style="font-size: 12pt;">搭乘捷運的話，坐到<span style="color: #0000ff;">捷運江子翠站</span>，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">從<strong>2號或4號出口</strong>出來後，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">過條馬路就到囉～</span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;<span style="font-size: 12pt;">餐點價位寫得清清楚楚～</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">主打販售麻油雞，也有麵線可選擇！</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/50675403182" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/50675403182_73ee9822d3_c.jpg" alt="IMG_20201202_165008"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">點餐時，老闆娘會詢問是否要加米酒，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">客製方式滿足不愛吃酒味太重的人！</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/50675319061" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/50675319061_bbd977be19_c.jpg" alt="IMG_20201202_172620"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 18.6667px; line-height: inherit; font-family: 微軟正黑體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #343434; letter-spacing: 1px; text-align: center;">▲</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 18.6667px; line-height: inherit; font-family: 微軟正黑體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #343434; letter-spacing: 1px; text-align: center; background-color: #ffff99;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: bold; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; text-decoration-line: underline;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 14pt; line-height: inherit; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">綜合麻油雞</span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">超豐盛的綜合麻油雞，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">裡頭配料什麼都有～</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">雞腿、雞塊、鳳翅、腰子、豬肝和高麗菜，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">大概是因為天氣太冷的關係，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">拍出來的照片好糊喔，哈哈哈</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/50675402707" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/50675402707_617c3744d8_c.jpg" alt="IMG_20201202_172807"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">每種雞肉吃起來非常新鮮，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">沒有太奇怪的味道，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">肉質鮮嫩、熟度相當完美！</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/50675402637" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/50675402637_dd30c5d777_c.jpg" alt="IMG_20201202_172901"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/50675319041" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/50675319041_9ee638a1db_c.jpg" alt="IMG_20201202_172656"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">豬肝給的也很多，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">至少有５塊左右，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">新鮮度也非常夠，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">沒有太明顯的腥味，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">完全滿足我這個愛吃豬肝的人😆</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/50675319031" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/50675319031_226f3861af_c.jpg" alt="IMG_20201202_172716"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/50675319001" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/50675319001_9d957aaab0_c.jpg" alt="IMG_20201202_172738"></a>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">麻油雞底下是滿滿的高麗菜，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">脆口又鮮甜～～</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/50675318856" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/50675318856_5b7ba660c5_c.jpg" alt="IMG_20201202_172917"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">再來，湯頭部分，是我最喜歡的❤️</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">每一口溫順又帶點淡淡的酒香，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">喝到最後也不覺得膩口，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">嘴裡還繚繞著麻油香氣，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">對於冬季想補身的人來說，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">會非常適合你喔！</span></p>
