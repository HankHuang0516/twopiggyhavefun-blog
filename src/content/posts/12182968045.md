<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #0000ff;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">** 喜歡旅遊、愛吃美食嗎？歡迎加入&nbsp;</span></span><span style="color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; text-decoration: underline;"><a style="color: #000000; text-decoration: underline;" href="https://www.facebook.com/twopiggyhavefun/?fref=ts" target="_blank"><span style="color: #ff0000; text-decoration: underline;">兩隻小豬到處吃喝玩樂遊記</span><span style="color: #ff0000; text-decoration: underline;">粉絲團</span></a></span><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #0000ff;">**</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">冬季進補時間到啦~~今天要教大家做這道「<strong>麻油雞湯</strong>」，讓你吃完手腳不再冰冷，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">趕快來看看怎麼做~~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;<img title="IMG_20151224_215043_504" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1451024455-1740308582.jpg" alt="IMG_20151224_215043_504" border="0"> &nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">準備食材：雞腿肉、麻油3大匙、香油1大匙、米酒1大匙、枸杞少許</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/054.gif" alt="" border="0">做法<span style="font-size: 16px;">：</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">1. 先準備一鍋水，水滾後放入雞肉川燙，再用冷水洗淨一次(備用)</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">2. 取一湯鍋，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">用中小火放入麻油3大匙+香油1大匙，慢慢煸香薑片至薑片變的焦香</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">3.&nbsp;放入川燙過的雞肉，炒至雞皮呈金黃狀態</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">4. 加入米酒<span style="font-size: 16px;">1大匙</span>，並加入酌量的水，蓋上鍋蓋用中小火燉煮約30分鐘</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">5.&nbsp;最後，再灑上少許枸杞即可</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">挖~~香噴噴的麻油雞湯完成囉<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/wink_smile.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">鍋蓋一打開，麻油味陣陣飄出來~~口感不油膩，味道蠻清爽的歐</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151224_215036_371" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1451022763-4197532047.jpg" alt="IMG_20151224_215036_371" border="0"> &nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">吃麻油雞，當然少不了一碗麵線~~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">準備食材：市售壽麵一包</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 16px;"><img style="font-size: 16px;" src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/054.gif" alt="" border="0">做法</span><span style="font-size: 16px;">：</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">1. 準備一湯鍋，倒入2碗水煮滾，再加入麵線，即完成。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/038.gif" alt="" border="0">小提醒：市售麵線已有鹹度，可依個人口味調整鹹淡!!</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151224_215047_760" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1451023664-2163637561.jpg" alt="IMG_20151224_215047_760" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">煮好的麵線，加點麻油雞湯一起吃，實在太讚啦~~</span></p>
