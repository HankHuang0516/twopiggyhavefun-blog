<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #0000ff;">包含：麵體、附加調味料、調味包及調味油包。</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43724313764" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1870/43724313764_8a8f073d38_z.jpg" alt="浣熊香辣海鮮炒烏龍麵 (7)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">首先，在鍋裡倒入６００ｍｌ的水煮沸～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29504010907" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1865/29504010907_bdc627b22a_z.jpg" alt="浣熊香辣海鮮炒烏龍麵 (10)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">放入麵體、附加調味料。</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43724312204" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1852/43724312204_6a74cb253b_z.jpg" alt="浣熊香辣海鮮炒烏龍麵 (11)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">烹煮５分鐘，且需不時攪拌喔～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43724311624" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1879/43724311624_af75caa2fe_z.jpg" alt="浣熊香辣海鮮炒烏龍麵 (12)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">我們還自己打了一顆蛋下去！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/30572820308" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1864/30572820308_f527b11b46_z.jpg" alt="浣熊香辣海鮮炒烏龍麵 (13)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">離開火源，留下約５湯匙的水。</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43724309914" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1867/43724309914_967c17dc81_z.jpg" alt="浣熊香辣海鮮炒烏龍麵 (15)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">重新打開爐火，加入調味包及調味油包</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/30572818788" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1890/30572818788_d464151e02_z.jpg" alt="浣熊香辣海鮮炒烏龍麵 (16)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">攪拌３０秒後即可享用～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44441948391" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1841/44441948391_53b120fe26_z.jpg" alt="浣熊香辣海鮮炒烏龍麵 (20)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">搭啦～<span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #ff6600;">「浣熊香辣海鮮炒烏龍麵」</span>完成啦！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29504001197" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1893/29504001197_e78f49d7b1_z.jpg" alt="浣熊香辣海鮮炒烏龍麵 (23)"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/30572817798" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1871/30572817798_9ee9256fb3_z.jpg" alt="浣熊香辣海鮮炒烏龍麵 (21)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">麵條本身屬於Q捲麵，幾乎吸附飽滿的海鮮滋味，相當濃郁～</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #ff0000;">以辣油炒出的香辣味道</span>，乾的吃法更顯得Q彈！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">而且吃起來不會覺得乾乾的，麵條有點吸水～</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">咀嚼在嘴裡，<span style="color: #993300;">辣度是會麻麻的</span>～而不是麻辣的口感！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">平常不太敢吃辣的人還在可接受範圍！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43724308114" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1850/43724308114_be99829077_z.jpg" alt="浣熊香辣海鮮炒烏龍麵 (25)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">裡頭還有浣熊造型魚板、昆布和海帶芽，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">讓人香辣到一口接一口～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29503997597" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1845/29503997597_62d2755622_z.jpg" alt="浣熊香辣海鮮炒烏龍麵 (26)"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43724315694" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1867/43724315694_747b0420f9_z.jpg" alt="浣熊香辣海鮮炒烏龍麵 (1)"></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">再來，要介紹另一款<span style="color: #ff6600; font-size: 14pt;">「香辣海鮮烏龍麵」</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這樣一大袋共有４入，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">裡面包含：麵體、附加調味料及調味包。</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43533022825" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1849/43533022825_dc4e3f23df_z.jpg" alt="香辣海鮮烏龍麵 (2)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">做法和前面一道差不多，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">只是這道是煮湯麵，所以減少把水倒掉的步驟～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43533021985" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1885/43533021985_c6c816f602_z.jpg" alt="香辣海鮮烏龍麵 (3)"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43533020705" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1849/43533020705_4d71041b7d_z.jpg" alt="香辣海鮮烏龍麵 (4)"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44441964211" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1868/44441964211_a3d91a0f27_z.jpg" alt="香辣海鮮烏龍麵 (5)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">首先，在５５０ｍｌ的沸水中，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">放入麵條、附加調味料及調味包。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">我們還另外加了芹菜提味～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43533023235" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1848/43533023235_8f2a28dab6_z.jpg" alt="香辣海鮮烏龍麵 (1)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">然後烹煮５分鐘，攪拌３０秒後關火～即可享用！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43533015045" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1871/43533015045_38262d951f_z.jpg" alt="香辣海鮮烏龍麵 (11)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這一款是煮成<span style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 14pt;">香辣海鮮湯麵</span>～</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">調味包的辣油是可以自行調整，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">也許是因為湯的吃法，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">所以麵條泡在湯裡偏Q軟，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">湯頭不會死鹹過辣！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43533013705" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1847/43533013705_db1d8a283e_z.jpg" alt="香辣海鮮烏龍麵 (13)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">雖然裡面沒有浣熊造型魚板，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但可以自己加入青菜、蛋，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">營養之外，也會很有飽足感唷～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43724316864" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1875/43724316864_009c8e513c_z.jpg" alt="香辣海鮮烏龍麵 (14)"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43724316144" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1851/43724316144_73a5612b36_z.jpg" alt="香辣海鮮烏龍麵 (15)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">總結：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">韓國泡麵最大特色就是麵體很Q、有嚼勁，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">炒烏龍麵和湯的吃法相較之下，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">兩種口感完全不同，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">我還是比較喜歡炒的口感，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">香辣濃郁程度是會讓人一吃就上癮～</span></p>
