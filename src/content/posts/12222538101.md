<div class="pixnet-article prose max-w-none">
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">發現宜蘭也有一座世外桃源，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">「仁山植物園」全程約三公里，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">終點是一個視野寬闊的觀景平台，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">很適合吃飽後散步、享受芬多精的好地方🍃</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43213347240" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1968/43213347240_75d6774496_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園 (1)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">仁山植物園</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">地址：宜蘭縣冬山鄉新寮路500號</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">門票：免費入園</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">門口有提供免費停車場</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #0000ff; text-decoration: underline;"><a href="https://renshan.e-land.gov.tw/Default.aspx" target="_blank"><span style="color: #0000ff; text-decoration: underline;">仁山植物園 官網&nbsp;</span></a></span></span><br></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">-</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">🔹FB/痞客邦🔎兩隻小豬🐷🐷</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">🔹IG🔎twopiggyhavefun</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">那天吃完 <span style="background-color: #ff99cc; color: #993366;"><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/blog/post/222489126" target="_blank"><span style="background-color: #ff99cc; color: #993366;">佐佐清水自家烘焙咖啡館</span></a> </span>後，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">發現附近有個「仁山植物園」，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">我們每次來宜蘭都沒去過，剛好順路就去那邊走走囉~</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31152094408" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1971/31152094408_3668b3a789_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園1"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">仁山植物園，位於中央山脈的最北端，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">冬山河的上遊處，海拔約300公尺，面積102公頃，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">是由宜蘭縣政府在此設置一苗圃，舊名中山苗圃，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">從原本的苗圃轉型為培育許多景觀及花草植物，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">更設置「仁山步道」，成為登山及休憩的絕佳去處。</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 10pt; color: #008080;">(取自蘭陽資訊網)</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31152094218" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1954/31152094218_80bf320700_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園2"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31152094038" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1971/31152094038_fe54c3e8da_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園3"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45024547181" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1925/45024547181_4f6f593b8e_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園4"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">「仁山植物園」平面配置圖</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這張地圖對我們來說非常重要阿~~(還好有事先拍起來)</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">看到文章後面就知道了 哈哈!!</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45024546911" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1903/45024546911_edd84f4747_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園5"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">上山的路共有兩條：一條是階梯步道(仁山步道)，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">另一條是公路步道，沿途可欣賞整片的茶園~</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45024546591" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1955/45024546591_7a2ae5ba90_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園6"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45024546331" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1933/45024546331_93e45fb1c0_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園7"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">看到路途標示的<span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #ff00ff;">「小心猴群」</span>警告標語，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">讓我內心不禁興奮，會不會走到一半有猴子半路殺出來XD</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45024546151" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1958/45024546151_2ffb47c530_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園8"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31152094688" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1912/31152094688_1b6f2e006a_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園 (11)"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">前面路段幾乎都是公路步道，路面稍微有坡度，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">終於看到一個可以坐下來休息地方<span style="color: #ff6600;">「新寮亭」</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45024545841" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1918/45024545841_15dfd71480_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園9"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">喝個水休息後再出發~</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">小豬發現走公路步道太慢，所以後半段路程我們改走「階梯步道」</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">走起來也會比走公路步道輕鬆~</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45024545591" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1928/45024545591_ee72384167_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園10"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">樟腦寮木炭窯是介紹祖先遺留下來的智慧，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">說明樟樹是如何煉取樟腦油，以及樟腦的功用及特性。</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45024545331" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1938/45024545331_de422b61dc_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園11"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #ff6600;">我的老天鵝阿~~~</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">走一大段路程後，總算親眼見到「法蘭西庭園」</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">幾何不規則狀的綠色植物，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">加上噴泉水池及女神雕像，更加凸顯出其特色。</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45024545021" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1934/45024545021_8cd96c027a_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園12"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44305403234" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1914/44305403234_9777ab651c_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園13"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43213347020" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1958/43213347020_31fb2ca282_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園14"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45024544771" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1911/45024544771_1a76dcc717_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園15"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45024544521" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1908/45024544521_d6ea6dec44_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園16"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45024544221" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1931/45024544221_5c0aec55b1_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園17"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45024543791" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1901/45024543791_4c4746257b_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園18"></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">紅色磚屋是「伊莉莎庭園」，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">夏季開放時間從8:00~17:30，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">當時爬上去時間已經是晚上6點，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">看起來暗暗的我們也不敢走進去XD</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44305402674" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1929/44305402674_29a2e0c05b_z.jpg" alt="仁山植物園19"></a></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">英式庭園植物展示區，外觀白色玻璃屋好夢幻呀~</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">太晚去門已經關了~所以只好在門口拍拍照囉!</span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">但站在純白玻璃屋門口拍也不錯～</span></p>
