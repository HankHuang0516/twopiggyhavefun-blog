<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #0000ff; font-family: 新細明體;">* 喜歡旅遊、</span><span style="letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #0000ff; font-family: 新細明體;">愛吃美食嗎？歡迎加入&nbsp;<span style="color: #777777; text-decoration: underline;"><a style="color: #000000; text-decoration: underline;" href="https://www.facebook.com/twopiggyhavefun/?fref=ts" target="_blank"><span style="color: #ff0000; text-decoration: underline;">兩隻小豬</span><span style="color: #ff0000; text-decoration: underline;">粉絲團</span></a></span>**</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">上個月平日休假時，和男友來到三峽滿月圓森林遊樂區~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">去滿月圓之前，中午我們安排來吃「<strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">台灣磚窯雞</span></strong>」，看網路上聲望還不錯，就來試試嚕!!</span></p>
<p><span style="letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 新細明體; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_20160406_123530_916" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1463016959-55013634.jpg" alt="IMG_20160406_123530_916" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">台灣磚窯雞位置很好找，就在大馬路旁~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">交通方式也非常方便，不論開車或騎車都有地方停，當天我們騎機車，就直接停店門口處囉</span></p>
<p><span style="letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 新細明體; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_20160406_123521_583" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1463016959-1018679709.jpg" alt="IMG_20160406_123521_583" border="0"></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">店門口旁有一區是專門在磚窯雞，看著磚窯人員烤的滿身大汗，突然明白粒粒皆辛苦的感覺<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/fox_07.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 新細明體; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_20160406_123653_415" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1463016959-606201971.jpg" alt="IMG_20160406_123653_415" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">門口處堆放了許多相思木，這是專門薰烤放山土雞用的</span></p>
<p><span style="letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 新細明體; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_20160406_123722_857" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1463016959-180400847.jpg" alt="IMG_20160406_123722_857" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">當天中午用餐時段，座位幾乎都是滿桌，還好我們有事先訂位，否則要站著等位子</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 新細明體; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;" title="IMG_20160406_123518_258" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1463016959-4147497797.jpg" alt="IMG_20160406_123518_258" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">店內環境寬敞明亮，座位區都以紅磚隔著，充滿古色古香~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 新細明體; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;" title="IMG_20160406_123549_286" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1463016959-2956426580.jpg" alt="IMG_20160406_123549_286" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">台灣磚窯雞MENU菜色非常豐富</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">除了有必點的磚窯烤全雞，還有各式蔬菜料理、海鮮料理、下酒菜及湯品</span></p>
<p><span style="letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 新細明體; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_20160406_123536_410" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1463016959-1984977183.jpg" alt="IMG_20160406_123536_410" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">更棒的是，這裡的<span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong>雞油拌飯是無限享用</strong></span>，牆上可以看到許多藝人簽名，由此可見這家算是大有名氣阿~~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img style="color: #0000ff; font-family: 新細明體; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;" title="IMG_20160406_123614_542" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1463016959-4211146222.jpg" alt="IMG_20160406_123614_542" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">牆上還有貼上雞油拌飯的食用方式，照著做就對啦</span></p>
<p><span style="letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 新細明體; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_20160406_123527_187" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1463016959-2246335829.jpg" alt="IMG_20160406_123527_187" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">碗筷、茶杯都是自取唷</span></p>
<p><span style="letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 新細明體; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_20160406_123541_069" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1463016959-3125455839.jpg" alt="IMG_20160406_123541_069" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">香噴噴的<span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong>雞油拌飯</strong></span>來囉~~充滿雞油的香味，真的太好吃了</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">等待磚窯烤雞送上來時，我們至少吃了2碗飯哈哈</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">雞油拌飯都這麼好吃，讓我已經開始期待磚窯烤雞了<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/shades_smile.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160406_123602_875" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1463018976-2596744303.jpg" alt="IMG_20160406_123602_875" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">將將將將~~~<span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong>磚窯烤全雞</strong></span>上桌囉<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/032.gif" alt="" border="0">&nbsp;$600元</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">整隻雞全身上下呈現金黃油亮亮，整個超誘人阿~~&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">還會附上泡菜、檸檬、胡椒粉和雞汁</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160406_123608_751" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1463018976-471433046.jpg" alt="IMG_20160406_123608_751" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">店家非常貼心，附上厚的手套給我們扒雞，比較不會燙手&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">油油亮亮的雞腿再沾上雞汁，肉質軟嫩又多汁<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/heart.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">個人覺得即使不沾醬，也非常夠味，可以單純吃出磚窯烤全雞的味道</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">可惜扒雞過程實在沒手拍照XD就沒有多拍幾張照片<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/tounge_smile.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160406_123622_147" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1463018976-416074266.jpg" alt="IMG_20160406_123622_147" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">季節時蔬</span></strong> $100元</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">由於我們人數比較少，就只點一道菜<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/whatchutalkingabout_smile.gif" alt="" border="0">還是會比較建議可以多人來，就可以吃到各種料理唷</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">翠綠的空心菜，加點蒜頭清炒就很好吃!!</span></p>

<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-family: 新細明體;">&nbsp;各位鄉親朋友們~~來到三峽，絕對不能錯過這家磚窯雞啊!!!</span></span></p>
<p><span style="letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000; font-family: 新細明體; font-size: 12pt;">店內環境寬敞，菜色非常豐富，超級適合家庭聚餐、朋友聚餐，下次一定要再來光顧~~</span></p>
