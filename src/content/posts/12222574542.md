<div class="pixnet-article prose max-w-none">
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #ff6600;">富民親水公園</span>位於基隆七堵區的瑪陵坑休閒遊憩區內，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">公園裡有瑪陵坑溪流，旁邊可以烤肉、玩水，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="background-color: #ffff99;">溪流乾淨又清澈</span>，還有好多小魚在裡面，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">是一個適合拍照、玩水的好地方!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44473163504" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1933/44473163504_5cc83d743b_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 01"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">富民親水公園</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">基隆市七堵區瑪陵坑</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Google map請定位在 <span style="background-color: #ffff99;">富民親水公園</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">-</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #0000ff;">🔹FB/痞客邦🔎兩隻小豬🐷🐷</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #0000ff;">🔹IG🔎twopiggyhavefun</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">第一次開車來到這裡，我們是定位在瑪陵坑，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">殊不知瑪陵坑這麼長一條，差點迷路…哈</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">所以，在這邊順便提醒大家，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">要前往的話，導航必須定位在<span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #ff0000;">富民親水公園</span>喔!!!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45145757462" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1915/45145757462_1504ec4111_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 02"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">富民橋跨越瑪陵坑溪，具有奇石、淺灘與深潭，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">成為遊客賞石、戲水與消暑遊憩的好地方!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31321450018" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1934/31321450018_01c87e1a0d_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 03"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">從樓梯往下走，就到達溪邊啦!!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44283898385" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1917/44283898385_d2521d2830_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 04"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45195251971" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1959/45195251971_5462837c43_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 05"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45195251311" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1906/45195251311_4a720077da_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 06"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">當天假日去的時候，人潮不算太多，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">旁邊有提供烤肉空間，還有涼亭，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">順便提醒大家:記得抱持公德心，不要在這留下垃圾唷!!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45195250721" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1922/45195250721_ccbea6b337_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 07"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這裡的溪水可以說是清澈見底<span style="font-size: 14pt; background-color: #ffff99;">(有圖有真相!!)</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">讓人想馬上拖鞋下去踩踩水~~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45195248691" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1950/45195248691_69389261f9_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 10"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45195250101" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1933/45195250101_4b8517c9e3_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 08"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">雙腳踩進水裡冰冰涼涼，溪水也很乾淨，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但還是要小心水裡的石頭會滑喔!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45195249221" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1903/45195249221_e748c23160_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 09"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">往前走一點，坐在石頭上方拍起來也不錯耶!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">潺潺流水加上綠色樹木，隨手拍就很有意境!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45195248161" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1929/45195248161_73e7f97ebe_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 11"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43381900900" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1931/43381900900_feec12f47c_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 12"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">來到這裡，讓我想到宜蘭的猴洞坑瀑布，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但不用像猴洞坑瀑布那麼辛苦，還要爬個10分鐘上去，哈</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43381899290" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1940/43381899290_f760bd21b2_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 16"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">除了有清澈溪水外，這裡的空氣也很棒，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">完全就是身在世外桃源的感覺…</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43381900040" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1928/43381900040_fe23c78fe0_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 14"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44473157824" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1975/44473157824_078579a767_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 15"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">特別近拍一張讓大家看看水裡的小魚悠游畫面，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">看來這裡的水質照顧得很好，才能養活這些水裡的小魚~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43381898850" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1902/43381898850_6bf352bf88_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 17"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">小豬的腳皮夠厚，馬上就有小魚來咬腳皮啦哈哈~~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43381898430" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1906/43381898430_37289e6bce_z.jpg" alt="富民橋親水空間 18"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">總結：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">平時上班覺得太厭世，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">真的很適合遠離都市來趟「富民親水公園」</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">這地方目前還不多人知道，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">就連小豬都說我是在哪找到這個地方，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">害我們差點在山裡迷路，哈哈哈</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">不僅空氣好、溪水清澈，還能拖鞋下去泡泡水，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">雙腳踩在水裡，馬上就有小魚來咬腳皮XD</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">是一個適合戲水消暑的好地方!</span></p>
