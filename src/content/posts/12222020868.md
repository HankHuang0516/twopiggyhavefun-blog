<p><span style="font-family: 新細明體, serif; font-size: 12pt;">這家「豆司苑」就位在中和南山路上，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: 新細明體, serif;">比較近的捷運站是南勢角站，但需要走幾分鐘的路程</span>!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: 新細明體, serif;">我們騎車過去很方便，巷子都有地方停</span><span style="font-family: 'Segoe UI Symbol', sans-serif;" lang="EN-US">!</span></span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/40915753490" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1733/40915753490_8cbab6db3f_z.jpg" alt="豆司苑 (17)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: 新細明體, serif;">室外座位區共有</span><span style="font-family: 'Segoe UI Symbol', sans-serif;" lang="EN-US">2</span><span style="font-family: 新細明體, serif;">桌，</span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-family: 新細明體, serif;">但天氣熱就沒選擇做室外</span><span style="font-family: 'Segoe UI Symbol', sans-serif;" lang="EN-US">!</span></span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41826419835" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1748/41826419835_7384756269_z.jpg" alt="豆司苑 (14)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">門口處有擺放菜單可供參考，</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">特別的是，還有一般早餐店不常見的韓式炒泡麵!!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41826421555" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1753/41826421555_36428c525e_z.jpg" alt="豆司苑 (18)"></a></p>
