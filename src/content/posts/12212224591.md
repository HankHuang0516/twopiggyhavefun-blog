<p><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 新細明體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #777777; letter-spacing: 1px;"><img style="color: #777777; font-family: 新細明體; letter-spacing: 1px;" title="IMG_0814" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440063-1187957989.jpg" alt="IMG_0814" border="0"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 新細明體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #777777; letter-spacing: 1px;">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">三重今大魯肉飯</span></strong></span><br><span style="font-size: 12pt;">地　　址： 新北市三重區大仁街40號</span><br><span style="font-size: 12pt;">聯絡電話： 02-29836726</span><br><span style="font-size: 12pt;">營業時間： 06:00~21:00（全年無休）</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">最近在網路看到好多人推薦這家三重「今大魯肉飯」，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">一直想來吃吃看，但我們比較少經過三重這邊，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">剛好那天打完保齡球，就跟小豬說可以來試試這家!!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">位在三重區大仁街上的「今大魯肉飯」果真不虛傳，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">是一家營數十年傳承至第二代的今大魯肉飯，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">停好機車時，就看到門口已經大排長龍<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/whatchutalkingabout_smile.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">可見今大魯肉飯頗具吸引力!!</span></p>
<p><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 新細明體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #777777; letter-spacing: 1px;"><img title="IMG_0800" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440063-1129483545.jpg" alt="IMG_0800" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 新細明體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #777777; letter-spacing: 1px;"><img title="IMG_0802" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440063-4281534958.jpg" alt="IMG_0802" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 新細明體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #777777; letter-spacing: 1px;"><img title="IMG_0804" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440063-3343386881.jpg" alt="IMG_0804" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">排了約7分鐘後，終於快到點餐區囉~~(興奮)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">每樣小菜都好吸引人，陷入選擇性障礙XD</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">而且一條吳郭魚竟然只要45元，會不會太便宜啦<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/omg_smile.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 新細明體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #777777; letter-spacing: 1px;"><img title="IMG_0805" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440063-1003850747.jpg" alt="IMG_0805" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">排隊時看著牆上的滷肉飯圖示，口水都快流下來啦~~</span></p>
<p><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 新細明體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #777777; letter-spacing: 1px;"><img title="IMG_0806" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440063-2900044047.jpg" alt="IMG_0806" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">終於快接近點餐區了~~</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">前方一大鍋的滷筍絲、滷豆腐超吸引人!!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">可惜滷豆腐剛下鍋煮，就沒有吃到...<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/cry_smile.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></span></p>
<p><img style="color: #777777; font-family: 新細明體; letter-spacing: 1px;" title="IMG_0812" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440063-3360843532.jpg" alt="IMG_0812" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 新細明體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #777777; letter-spacing: 1px;"><img title="IMG_0809" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440063-2170531910.jpg" alt="IMG_0809" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">兩隻小豬看到每樣小菜都好好吃的感覺，幾乎點了一整桌~~</span></p>
<p><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 新細明體; vertical-align: baseline; color: #777777; letter-spacing: 1px;"><img title="IMG_0813" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440063-2508111144.jpg" alt="IMG_0813" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">鲁肉飯 $25元</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">使用雞骨、豬骨熬的湯頭做肉燥湯底，在白飯淋上滿滿的肥肉燥，整個快蓋住白飯了~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">一般魯肉飯都是肥肉和瘦肉比例適中，但這裡全是肥肉!!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">雖然看似有點油膩，但吃起來肥而不膩，沒有噁心反胃的感覺~</span></p>
<p><img style="color: #777777; font-family: 新細明體; letter-spacing: 1px;" title="IMG_0814" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440063-1187957989.jpg" alt="IMG_0814" border="0"><span style="color: #777777; font-family: 新細明體; letter-spacing: 1px;">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong><span style="font-family: 新細明體; letter-spacing: 1px; font-size: 12pt;">鲁白菜 $25元</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: 新細明體; letter-spacing: 1px; font-size: 12pt;">小菜部分，個人覺得蠻普通，就不多做介紹了~</span></p>
<p><img title="IMG_0815" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440249-967628576.jpg" alt="IMG_0815" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong><span style="font-family: 新細明體; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px;">鲁筍絲 $25元</span></strong></span></p>
<p><img title="IMG_0816" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440249-2050564207.jpg" alt="IMG_0816" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">魯蛋 $10元</span></p>
<p><img title="IMG_0818" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440249-1259308838.jpg" alt="IMG_0818" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">魯蛋外皮口感脆Q，但裡面蛋黃偏乾，剛好搭配一旁的香菇排骨湯</span></p>
<p><img title="IMG_0822" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440249-1850843314.jpg" alt="IMG_0822" border="0"></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">吳郭魚 $45元</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">沒想到在今大魯肉飯竟然能吃到完整的吳郭魚!!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">而且裡面的魚肉也算多，只是不小心會吃到小小的魚刺...</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">提醒大家，吃魚的時候還是要專心吃!!</span></p>
<p><img title="IMG_0820" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440249-1255345689.jpg" alt="IMG_0820" border="0"></p>
<p><img title="IMG_0821" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440249-1819672349.jpg" alt="IMG_0821" border="0">\</p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600;"><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">香菇排骨湯 $40元</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">小小一碗的香菇排骨湯裡，居然有幾顆蛤罵、排骨、香菇，湯頭鮮味十足!</span></p>
<p><img title="IMG_0824" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1481440249-12010382.jpg" alt="IMG_0824" border="0"> &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">雖然這家今大魯肉飯離住的地方較遠，又要排隊、環境也沒有很好，<br></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但<span style="font-size: 16px;">魯肉給的很豪邁，</span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span>超級噴香美味，兩人一餐吃下來只要210元，</span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span>已經列入我的最愛清單中XD</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">身為滷肉飯控的人，一定要來嘗鮮歐~~~&nbsp;</span></p>
