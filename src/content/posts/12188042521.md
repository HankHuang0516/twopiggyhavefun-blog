<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #0000ff;">** 喜歡旅遊、愛吃美食嗎？歡迎加入&nbsp;</span><span style="color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; text-decoration: underline;"><a style="color: #000000; text-decoration: underline;" href="https://www.facebook.com/twopiggyhavefun/?fref=ts" target="_blank"><span style="color: #ff0000; text-decoration: underline;">兩隻小豬</span><span style="color: #ff0000; text-decoration: underline;">粉絲團</span></a></span><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #0000ff;">**</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><img title="page" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1453952095-4240036848.jpg" alt="page" border="0"> &nbsp;<br></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">有全世界最好吃爆米花美名的「<span style="color: #ff0000;">Garrett POPCORN</span>」終於在台北101開幕囉！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">「Garrett 爆米花」是以手工製作聞名，每一批爆米花不是油炸，而是經過氣炸處理，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">堅持以不添加防腐劑的天然原料製作，讓大家吃得健康~</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: arial;"><span style="font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">2016/1/28這天下班後，吃完飯馬上和男朋友來購買，</span></span><span style="font-family: arial;"><span style="font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">沒想到排隊人潮真是強強滾~~</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">排隊區其實在Apple 門市的中間區塊，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">等候的同時店員會先發一章點單券，並限制每人最多只能買兩罐+兩袋。</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><img style="font-family: arial; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;" title="IMG_20160127_224845_170" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1453952291-2300700619.jpg" alt="IMG_20160127_224845_170" border="0"></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">價目表請參考下方圖片~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">雖然價位有點貴，但真的是好吃阿~~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">口味總共有7種，包含<strong>芝加哥招牌混搭、酥脆焦糖、香濃起司、濃郁奶油、原味、杏仁酥脆焦糖、夏威夷果酥脆焦糖，</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">購買時可以選擇要桶裝或是袋裝~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">無論是桶裝或是紙袋裝，都有三種大小可以選擇!!</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">過年期間如果想吸引小朋友的目光，可愛的猴年新年限定桶肯定會是個送禮好選擇喔！</span></p>
