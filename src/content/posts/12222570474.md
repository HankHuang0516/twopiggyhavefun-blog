<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">包含里肌肉、生菜沙拉、紅藜麥，上層淋上誘人的起司醬</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">由於內餡有再加煎蛋，所以口感更加濕潤~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">而每塊吐司之間都有塗上花生醬，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">口味非常開胃，生菜部分也很新鮮，吃完真的超飽足!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44449419604" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1963/44449419604_374605b761_z.jpg" alt="MORNING GO Brunch16"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">你看看~裡面配料豐富成這樣，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">以這價位來說，可以吃到這麼新鮮美味的餐點，真的很超值!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/30233185527" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1956/30233185527_98cc3d2894_z.jpg" alt="MORNING GO Brunch (5)"></a></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">一旁還有附上一小碗芋頭塊，可以自行加在吐司裡頭唷!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43357510770" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1974/43357510770_56ecf45b0f_z.jpg" alt="MORNING GO Brunch19"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">吐司夾層包裹著滿滿的芋泥，看得好療癒阿~~~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">咬下一口~可以明顯吃到芋頭的顆粒感，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">芋泥綿密滑順，不會太甜，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">光是吃完半份，就大約有5分飽了!</span></p>

<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31298079848" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1915/31298079848_8e285767cf_z.jpg" alt="MORNING GO Brunch21"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/30233184987" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1949/30233184987_0744158882_z.jpg" alt="MORNING GO Brunch (34)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">搗Z厚里肌吐司 (紅藜賣沙拉+熔岩起司 $105元)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">第一次吃到有加入豆腐乳的土司三明治，非常新奇!!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">通常三明治本身的香味靠的是肉片與起司，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但摻入了經過發酵後的豆腐乳，便提升了味覺的層次，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">從口中化出酸鹹香味，自然帶出胃口，小豬非常推薦唷!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45122712912" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1943/45122712912_35bfe9323a_z.jpg" alt="MORNING GO Brunch23"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">經典粉漿蛋餅 (花醬棉花糖+$30元) $65元</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">沒想到蛋餅、花生醬和棉花糖搭配在一塊可以這麼MATCH!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">蛋餅皮和在外面早餐店吃到的很不一樣，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">餅皮大家可以先想像成像板條口感，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">整體吃起來甜鹹甜鹹的滋味，完全無違和感阿!!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44449418514" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1953/44449418514_abdcac46a3_z.jpg" alt="MORNING GO Brunch25"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/45122712592" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1912/45122712592_aea99fa08f_z.jpg" alt="MORNING GO Brunch26"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="background-color: #ccffcc;">左/紅茶歐蕾</span>，<span style="background-color: #ccffff;">右/芋頭鮮奶</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">紅茶歐蕾 $60元</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這裡的茶葉是使用台北知名的林華泰茶行，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">味道絕對和一般早餐點不一樣!!!!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這杯紅茶歐蕾甜度不高，味道微甜順口~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">-</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">每日限定飲品(芋頭鮮奶) $70元</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">身為芋頭控當然要點杯芋頭鮮奶試試阿!!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">底層芋頭泥口感帶點水份，可以吸到芋頭顆粒感，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">喝起來甜度和口感都剛剛好!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/44449418394" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1976/44449418394_bb3458d126_z.jpg" alt="MORNING GO Brunch27"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">總結：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">「MORNING GO Brunch」餐點製作用心又新鮮，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">三明治口味也很有特色，像是：<span style="background-color: #ffff99;">搗Z厚里肌吐司</span>，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">吐司本身就帶有鮮奶香氣，摻入了經過發酵後的豆腐乳，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">整體口味酸鹹好滋味，原來吐司也可以這麼多變化～</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">而<span style="color: #ff6600;">「手作蜜糖芋泥吐司」</span>也很推薦大家點！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">芋泥本身是顆粒口感，可以吃的到芋頭塊，口味也不會太甜～</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">每種餐點價位也在可接受範圍內，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">以平實價格就能吃到用料實在的早午餐，</span></p>
