<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">黃金泡菜 $60</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54209764204" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" title="新莊聚餐 廣泰樓小館" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54209764204_e3a596eac2_c.jpg" alt="新莊聚餐 廣泰樓小館"></a></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">蒜香雲耳 $80</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">吃起來香脆的黑木耳，充滿著滑潤感，還有融入蒜味的酸甜醬汁，既開胃又提味，小孩接受度也高。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54208628217" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" title="新莊聚餐 廣泰樓小館" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54208628217_f4515bae4f_c.jpg" alt="新莊聚餐 廣泰樓小館"></a></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">桂花蜜芋頭 $100</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這道蜜芋頭真的好綿密，每一口都有桂花的自然甜味，甜而不膩好入口，綿密中還帶著微微的粉糯，風味細膩有層次。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54209528666" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" title="新莊聚餐 廣泰樓小館" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54209528666_a7d2e63d59_c.jpg" alt="新莊聚餐 廣泰樓小館"></a></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">蒜香脆皮雞/半隻 $540</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">來到廣泰樓小館，一定不能錯過他們家的招牌功夫菜<span style="color: #993300;">蒜香脆皮雞</span>！這道菜光是製作過程就讓人驚嘆，據說耗時整整一天才能完成。</span>&nbsp;</p>
<p><a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54209742659" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" title="新莊聚餐 廣泰樓小館" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54209742659_ea58ec8590_c.jpg" alt="新莊聚餐 廣泰樓小館"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">選用兩斤半的台東紅羽雞，從醃漬開始就相當講究，加入中藥提味，再經過淋醋水、風乾、淋油搶皮等繁複工序。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">不過這可不是一般炸雞喔！脆皮雞的重點在於精緻的技術和對火候的掌握。皮必須酥脆，但絕不能焦黑，每個細節都極其考驗廚師的功力。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a style="font-size: 16px;" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54209742899" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" title="新莊聚餐 廣泰樓小館" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54209742899_5ce417c33b_c.jpg" alt="新莊聚餐 廣泰樓小館"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">咬下一口，那層金黃酥脆的外皮會在齒間「卡滋」一聲，雞肉軟嫩卻不失彈性，肉質鮮嫩多汁好滋味，還帶著中藥醃漬的微微甘香，每一口都彷彿在品嚐師傅的用心。</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">香酥芋頭鴨 $415</span></p>

<p><a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54209520191" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" title="新莊聚餐 廣泰樓小館" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54209520191_c83e7f06e8_c.jpg" alt="新莊聚餐 廣泰樓小館"></a></p>

<p><a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54209761898" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" title="新莊聚餐 廣泰樓小館" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54209761898_37641aa78d_c.jpg" alt="新莊聚餐 廣泰樓小館"></a></p>
