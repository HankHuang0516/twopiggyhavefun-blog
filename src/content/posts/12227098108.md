<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">還有一整面的兩棲爬蟲區，有超可愛的鬆獅蜥、豹紋守宮、變色龍及樹蛙，小朋友們看得目不轉睛。</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #888888;">蟲蟲巴斯｜顯微鏡觀察課程</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54039464710" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54039464710_3b02f3a414_c.jpg" alt="IMG_8044"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這次幫兒子報名的是<span style="color: #008080;">顯微鏡觀察課程</span>，主要以遊戲的方式進行課程，從操作顯微鏡、觀察標本再繪製紀錄昆蟲的各種特徵，結合實驗跟手作體驗。</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54038150857" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54038150857_97e0186483_c.jpg" alt="IMG_8046"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">顯微鏡觀察課一堂是90分鐘，適合5歲以上小朋友，大人可以陪同。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">想要當個小小昆蟲學家，學習使用顯微鏡是很重要的，跟著森森老師一起找出蟲蟲通緝犯吧！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54038150807" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54038150807_caf8ba34cd_c.jpg" alt="IMG_8048"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">首先，森森老師會教小朋友們<strong>如何操作顯微鏡</strong>，<strong>調整調節輪和焦距</strong>。因為有的昆蟲體型比較小，肉眼難觀察外表，有顯微鏡當作輔助是很重要的！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54039342209" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54039342209_16c30cefda_c.jpg" alt="photo-output(2)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">觀察昆蟲前，老師讓我們拿出百鈔在顯微鏡下觀察，</span>原來100元上面還有好多小小數字100，以前都沒發現耶😆</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54039464285" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54039464285_8274094e31_c.jpg" alt="IMG_8057"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">接下來每個人會拿到一張<strong>蟲蟲檔案卡</strong>，上面有很多不同圖形的昆蟲，破解後就能拿到屬於自己的蟲蟲檔案囉！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54038150532" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54038150532_8850099d43_c.jpg" alt="IMG_8058"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54039463890" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54039463890_55962a0281_c.jpg" alt="IMG_8072"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54039270323" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54039270323_af14dd6d29_c.jpg" alt="IMG_8071"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">我們拿到的標本是偷菜賊：<strong><span style="color: #993300;">螽斯</span></strong>，後足明顯比前中足粗，非常好辨認的特徵，然後再根據顯微鏡上看到的樣貌畫下來。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54039010996" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54039010996_88f716068c_c.jpg" alt="photo-output(1)"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">有沒有發現，螽斯細長的觸角比身體還長呢！後腳部分也很細長。</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54039461885" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54039461885_1e70334df4_c.jpg" alt="photo-output(3)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">最後，森森老師會依序介紹<strong>椿象</strong>、<strong>蠼螋</strong>、<strong>螳螂</strong>、<strong>螽斯</strong>和<strong>天牛</strong>的特徵及食性。跟孩子在旁邊上課，自己也學到很多昆蟲知識😍</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/54039462950" target="_blank"><img loading="lazy" src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54039462950_320b6f0992_c.jpg" alt="IMG_8121"></a></p>
