<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">大家注意，內用和外帶的排隊是不同條喔</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">通常內用都是排在最左邊!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29734553277" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1875/29734553277_ebe3556981_z.jpg" alt="彰化肉圓 (9)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">提早下班來排隊是對的，哈哈</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這回總算讓我吃到肉圓啦!!!!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29734554507" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1865/29734554507_2f5d3b9f08_z.jpg" alt="彰化肉圓 (2)"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29734553567" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1855/29734553567_3796d76169_z.jpg" alt="彰化肉圓 (7)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">肉圓 大份$80元/2顆</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">肉圓是以蒸熟再油炸的方式，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">外皮部分相當軟嫩，還不到Q彈口感，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">裏頭的豬肉很大塊，口感較紮實，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">還有不少脆口的筍絲，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">搭配特製的甜醬，別有一番風味!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">別忘了吃到最後，可以找老闆加清湯，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">就可以省下一碗湯的錢啦哈哈</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/29734554137" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1852/29734554137_e6cb711dcf_z.jpg" alt="彰化肉圓 (4)"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/43953718734" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1875/43953718734_8f09a0d552_z.jpg" alt="彰化肉圓 (6)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">總結：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">肉圓可以說是我個人非常愛的台灣小吃之一，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">通常分成分成蒸、炸兩種，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">而這家「安東街彰化肉圓」是以蒸熟再油炸的方式，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">外皮部分特別軟嫩，搭配鹹鹹甜甜的特調醬汁，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">吃完肉圓，底層醬汁加上免費清湯，晚餐就解決啦!</span></p>
