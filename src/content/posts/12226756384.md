<p><span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #888888;">林記肉圓｜店家資訊</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233604545" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233604545_bf4458a24e_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓30"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">「林記肉圓」靠近英專路和水源路一段這邊，從淡水捷運站步行約6分鐘即可抵達；開車的朋友，可以停在附近的「好停仁愛停車場」，走過去很快就到囉～</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這家林記肉圓開業至今已有23年了，營業時間從中午12點一路開到晚上9點，不論是午餐還是晚餐，都能享受美味的小吃！而且<span style="color: #008080;">GOOGLE評論數多達341則有4.6高分</span>，是陪伴不少在地人從小吃到大的美食小吃呢！</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #888888;"><span>林記肉圓｜店內環境&amp;菜單價位</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #888888;"><span style="color: #888888;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233604540" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233604540_ddaf16e634_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓27"></a></span></span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233103281" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233103281_cd2f3c4501_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓29"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">菜單全寫在攤車前的板上，主要販售肉圓、肉粽、油粿和四神湯，價格約$45～$80，這樣有點小貴的感覺，但一試之後你會覺得，真的物超所值！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53232237267" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53232237267_cdd6bc170e_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓28"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">店內用餐區不大，裡頭擺了數張桌椅讓客人可以內用，很接地氣的小吃環境，但是座位區都整理得很乾淨不會有濕黏的感覺～</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233103396" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233103396_7104338484_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓26"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">最近這種大熱天去用餐也不會吃得滿身汗，因為店裡有冷氣可吹，超貼心😃另外，中午時段去用餐，看到蠻多客人都是外帶回家回家享用，生意很好，老闆人也很親切，很加分！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233605055" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233605055_03aae29e42_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓1"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">這邊肉圓兩顆賣$70，份量還算蠻大顆的，下午解解饞剛剛好！肉圓是先蒸過再現場使用低溫油泡，讓外皮保持Q軟的口感～油的部分也很乾淨透明。</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233406003" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233406003_b45912a224_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓23"></a></p>

<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233103656" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233103656_eff23e150c_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓5"></a></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">肉圓外皮是相當Q彈又帶點軟的咬勁，淋上店家自製的在來米醬跟蒜蓉醬油，整個非常對味！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233103596" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233103596_9d5dafe4a0_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓10"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233482394" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233482394_342a57001b_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓19"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">而且肉餡中還夾帶許多筍塊，軟中帶脆很有層次感，清爽不膩口，正好是我喜歡的滋味～很容易讓人一顆接一顆吃！</span>&nbsp;</p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233406258" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233406258_55080c484b_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓6"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">喜愛吃辣的朋友們，可以再加上辣醬一起吃，口感更豐富喔！</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #ff6600;">肉粽 $65</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><br></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #ff6600;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233482424" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233482424_c0e3b57d4e_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓18"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233482439" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233482439_d270b4828b_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓14"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">除了肉圓之外，這裡的肉粽也很強喔！當用筷子把肉粽剖開時，可以感覺糯米已經煮到軟綿，是我們愛的口感❤️配料有蛋黃、三層肉、栗子、香菇和花生，調味上不會太重，糯米軟硬適中，淋上在來米醬和蒜蓉醬油，滋味鹹甜交錯超滿足！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233103581" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233103581_4b573f13b4_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓12"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">肉粽吃起來就真的是很傳統味道，有我喜歡的鹹蛋黃很加分，整體口感與香氣更提升，內餡也很豐富，吃起來很過癮😝</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #ff6600;">油粿 $45</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233406123" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233406123_1a4e3d755d_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓17"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">另一道油粿也是必嚐之一！之前在別家吃到的油粿都偏乾，但林記的油粿意外的超好吃！</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233604760" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233604760_06487bdec3_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓20"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span>油粿本身鬆軟又Q彈，輕咬幾下馬上就化開，加上店家獨特的醬汁非常特別～</span></span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233604945" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233604945_b14262168a_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓9"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">裡面還有加入切丁的芋頭，相當入口即化好喜歡😍整體還有濃厚的芋頭香氣，喜愛芋頭的人一定要點！</span></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #ff6600;">豬肚湯 $80</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233103686" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233103686_7ac52f601a_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓2"></a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">料多實在的豬肚湯，料非常多喔！怎麼撈都有料，豬肚吃起來緊實有咬勁，湯頭偏清爽，口味不重小孩也愛喝！</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233406138" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233406138_5536ae6121_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓16"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;">裡頭還有不少薏仁，整碗喝完覺得超飽足，老闆還跟我們說內用可免費加湯哦🫶🏻</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><br class="Apple-interchange-newline"><span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #ff6600;">綜合湯（腸+肚） $80</span></span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/53233604985" target="_blank"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53233604985_6135d5dd61_c.jpg" alt="淡水林記肉圓4"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">一樣是有滿滿配料的綜合湯，加了豬肚和豬腸兩種，豬腸清理得很乾淨不會太難嚼，湯頭順喉好喝，碗內豬肚和豬腸給滿滿，吃完肉圓來一碗解膩剛剛好！</span></p>
