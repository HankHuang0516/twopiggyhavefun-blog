<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #0000ff;">** 喜歡旅遊、愛吃美食嗎？歡迎加入&nbsp;</span><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a style="color: #000000; text-decoration: underline;" href="https://www.facebook.com/twopiggyhavefun/?fref=ts" target="_blank"><span style="color: #ff0000; text-decoration: underline;">兩隻小豬</span><span style="color: #ff0000; text-decoration: underline;">粉絲團</span></a></span><span style="color: #0000ff;">**</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="page" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459656551-1936279710.jpg" alt="page" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;在香港第一天的晚餐，我們來到「橋底辣蟹」用餐~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;這家是正宗避風塘辣蟹專門店，也是香港旅遊的熱門店家之一!!</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;而且交通非常方便，搭乘香港地鐵到銅鑼灣站，步行約5分鐘就到囉~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5346" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459656551-1936974354.jpg" alt="IMG_5346" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;一走進來，充滿一種港式味道~(廢話!!這是香港阿XD)</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5348" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459656551-1204470077.jpg" alt="IMG_5348" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">由於一樓座位已經坐滿滿，我們被安排到2樓用餐&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5349" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459656551-3041170252.jpg" alt="IMG_5349" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5356" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459657870-2954322429.jpg" alt="IMG_5356" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">我們人數眾多，坐在10人座的圓桌~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5350" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459656551-1840377437.jpg" alt="IMG_5350" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;一坐下來，已經迫不期待要大吃辣蟹啦~~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5351" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459656551-3895152491.jpg" alt="IMG_5351" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">這邊的衛生紙包裝好特別，都是菜單照片</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5352" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459656551-2600063757.jpg" alt="IMG_5352" border="0"> &nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600; font-size: 12pt;"><strong>一品燒味拼盤</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">吃到這道燒臘，讓我不禁懷念起台灣燒臘店的便當~))想家....<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/cry_smile.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">個人最喜歡吃的就是油雞，肉質非常軟嫩，搭配皮一起吃，超級好吃~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5354" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459657870-1860082485.jpg" alt="IMG_5354" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600; font-size: 12pt;"><strong>招牌橋底炒飯</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">炒飯的米粒炒至粒粒分明，口感非常清爽不油膩~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5357" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459657870-1301591716.jpg" alt="IMG_5357" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5358" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459657870-1165878674.jpg" alt="IMG_5358" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">我忘記這道叫什麼~但有點類似家中會煮的蛤蜊湯，只是裡頭多加了冬粉和海瓜子，鮮味十足!!</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5359" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459657870-1095849847.jpg" alt="IMG_5359" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">個人覺得湯頭喝起來很鮮甜~等待炒蟹送上來時，我已經裝了好幾碗喝~~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5360" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459657870-4133028189.jpg" alt="IMG_5360" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">這道魚雖然魚刺有點多，但肉質新鮮好吃，搭配底層的調味醬一起吃，非常夠味~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5361" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459657870-2037138407.jpg" alt="IMG_5361" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600; font-size: 12pt;"><strong>清炒時蔬</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">裡面有花枝和花椰菜，清爽又健康的一道菜~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5362" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459657870-2684358943.jpg" alt="IMG_5362" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600; font-size: 12pt;"><strong>薑絲竹貝</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #000000;">原本以為這道是大腸之類的，但其實是貝類料理，</span>還滿好吃唷~~非常Q軟鮮美</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5363" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459657870-3397844161.jpg" alt="IMG_5363" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600; font-size: 12pt;"><strong>蒜酥蝦</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">蝦子肉質新鮮，還加了超多的蒜香，超級讚啦~~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5366" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459657870-2595702553.jpg" alt="IMG_5366" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #ff6600; font-size: 12pt;"><strong>清炒時蔬</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">這道結合了大白菜和香菇，口感清爽不油膩~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5367" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459657870-2610615567.jpg" alt="IMG_5367" border="0"> &nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">重頭戲~~<strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">《橋底辣蟹》</span></strong>來囉~~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">雖然這道稱為辣蟹，但辣度可以做調整唷~我們吃的是微辣，個人還可以接受~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5368" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459660249-3378229735.jpg" alt="IMG_5368" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">被滿滿蒜末掩沒的辣蟹，真的炒得非常香~~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5369" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459660249-939275828.jpg" alt="IMG_5369" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5370" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459660249-164244731.jpg" alt="IMG_5370" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">蟹腳真的是巨無霸耶!!!店家非常貼心，有準備一支專門剪蟹腳的剪刀~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">外殼剪碎後，全是滿滿的白色蟹肉，肉質結實又入味!!</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">因為當時手太忙碌，就沒有拍蟹肉的照片，請大家見諒餒~~</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_5371" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1459660249-3810373318.jpg" alt="IMG_5371" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">這餐吃下來，總共有9道菜，每道菜都很像台式料理，讓人不禁懷念起台灣味，哈哈</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">最推薦的還是《橋底辣蟹》，第一次看到在辣蟹上加滿蒜末，簡直快將蟹腳淹沒XD</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">愛吃螃蟹的朋友們，來到香港一定要來這家報到喔~~</span></p>
