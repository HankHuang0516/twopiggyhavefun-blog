<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">台灣的國民美食之一就是牛肉麵啦<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/032.gif" alt="" border="0">幾乎走在街頭巷尾都可以看見不少牛肉麵店家。</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">位於台北市濟南路上的</span><strong><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #ff0000;">七十二牛肉麵</span></strong><span style="font-size: 16px;">，招牌上寫著大紅色的「七十二牛肉麵」非常好找</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140818_124" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316248-1851105595.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140818_124" border="0">&nbsp; '</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">這家牛肉麵店竟然連續兩年入榜美國美食網站「每日一餐」(The Daily Meal)網站評比的「<span style="color: #0000ff;">亞洲最佳101家美食餐廳</span>」</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #000000;"><img style="font-size: 16px;" title="IMG_20151107_140756_327" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316346-3525087007.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140756_327" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">店名取為七十二牛肉麵，是因為他的清燉湯頭是用牛大骨熬煮72小時</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 16px;">門口就擺上用過的牛大骨~</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 16px;"><img style="font-size: 16px;" title="IMG_20151107_140805_950" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316346-1825249464.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140805_950" border="0"></span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">還有榮獲臺版米其林評鑑四顆星<img style="font-size: 16px;" src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/043.gif" alt="" border="0"><img style="font-size: 16px;" src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/043.gif" alt="" border="0"><img style="font-size: 16px;" src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/043.gif" alt="" border="0"><img style="font-size: 16px;" src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/043.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140750_972" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316346-1938683228.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140750_972" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">外觀及用餐環境跟台灣多數的牛肉麵店並沒有太大的不同</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140657_729" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316346-3380194066.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140657_729" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">用餐這天是平日晚餐來，蠻多學生族群，應該是忠孝新生附近的北科同學~</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140713_150" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316346-699944020.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140713_150" border="0"> &nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">兩隻小豬點了一碗清燉牛肉麵、半筋半肉麵及皮蛋豆腐</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">如果是北科同學，點餐時記得和服務人員說一下，會送一碗美味的花干唷((灑花</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140644_910" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316780-59956212.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140644_910" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">店裡還有花干卡可以買，一張50元，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">可以免費吃一年牛滷花干唷&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140648_555" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316780-1167808223.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140648_555" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">七十二牛肉麵的皮蛋很大顆，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">豆腐吃起來很嫩、口感細緻~</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140700_186" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316780-1759481676.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140700_186" border="0"><br></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">免費贈送的花干，<span style="font-size: 16px;">花干口感軟嫩，</span></span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">一人吃份量算多</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140716_597" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316780-3747094120.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140716_597" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">吃花干時，可以加上旁邊的牛骨髓醬</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140718_382" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316780-2288288130.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140718_382" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">半筋半肉麵</span></strong><img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/032.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">牛肉麵要好吃，麵條、湯頭及牛肉三大元素缺一不可，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">湯頭更是牛肉麵的精華，要做出好吃牛肉麵可不簡單。</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">七十二牛肉麵的牛肉湯頭絕對不加味精，全都是靠牛肉及蔬菜的甜味。</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">這湯頭熬煮72小時之後顏色會轉為乳白色，就是七十二牛肉麵清燉湯頭，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">因為每次只燉煮一鍋，所以一天只能賣出80多碗。</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140721_098" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316780-612852766.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140721_098" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">牛肉吃起來非常軟嫩</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140728_076" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316780-4110826828.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140728_076" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">牛筋一口咬下，帶有Q彈嚼勁！</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140733_678" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316780-2906738711.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140733_678" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #000000;"><strong><span style="color: #ff6600;">清燉牛肉麵</span></strong><img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/032.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">湯頭看起來真的很清燉!!</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140736_694" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316780-2194334182.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140736_694" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">牛肉份量不多，大概3~4片，肉質有點乾乾的((個人不是很愛</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140739_251" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316780-3884295226.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140739_251" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">湯頭非常清淡，如果比較重口味，建議可以點紅燒口味~</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140742_894" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316780-4120294795.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140742_894" border="0"></span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">帳單出來啦~兩隻小豬吃下來總共400元，實在不便宜阿~~</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20151107_140745_154" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1447316780-947920641.jpg" alt="IMG_20151107_140745_154" border="0">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">從店名中就可以知道，七十二牛肉麵的湯頭是經過了三天三夜的熬製，並強調絕無滲水，</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">一斤半的牛骨才煮出一碗湯，精華全在其中~~</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">愛吃牛肉麵的朋友們千萬不要錯過，趕</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">快來嘗鮮吧！</span></span></p>
