<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41849078875" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1730/41849078875_de751e3874_z.jpg" alt="冰雪湯冰舖 (13)"></a></p>

<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/28875841118" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1750/28875841118_64b370559a_z.jpg" alt="冰雪湯冰舖 (14)"></a></p>

<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/41849078615" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1748/41849078615_9ee9b6800a_z.jpg" alt="冰雪湯冰舖 (15)"></a></p>

<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/28875840608" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1757/28875840608_0143de4272_z.jpg" alt="冰雪湯冰舖 (16)"></a></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">個人是覺得蠻普通啦！</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">但我覺得芋頭吃起來偏硬，</span></p>
