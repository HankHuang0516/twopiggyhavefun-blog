<p>&nbsp;<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #0000ff; text-decoration: underline;"><a style="text-decoration: underline; color: #020202;" href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/blog/post/82031214"><span style="color: #0000ff; text-decoration: underline;">*教學*Arduino WIFI(ESP8266) 初次上手應用</span></a></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">簡單幾個步驟就能把感測器上傳到Thinkspeak資料庫，方便使用者監視。</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">這次我們要準備的材料有:</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">--------------------WIFI模組應用材料--------------------</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">軟體:</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">Arduino IDE</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">Realterm(WIN10用終端機)&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">硬體:</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">WIFI數據機 or 手機熱點分享&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">Arduino 板子 (Uno、Nano、Mega、Mini、Yun...都可) *1塊</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">FT232 &nbsp;*1塊</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">ESP8266 &nbsp;(鮑率9600)*1塊</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">--------------------------------------------------------------</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">如果你是從初次上手一路學過來的，實驗方法1跟2都是前置作業，完全一樣，可以直接跳到3開始。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><strong>實驗方法</strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #ff6600;">1.先用Realterm(終端機)測試ESP8266是否正常。(老話一句 如果正常本次實驗就成功80%了~!)</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #ff6600;">2.再用Arduino取代Realterm(終端機)與ESP8266溝通，開內建序列監控室窗進行測試。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #ff6600;">3.輸入查找IP指令取得IP位址。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #ff6600;">4.下載本實驗需要用到的檔案，並燒錄本實驗的code</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #ff6600;">5.<span style="color: #ff6600; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">建構網頁，並修改html內碼。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #ff6600;"><span style="color: #ff6600; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">6.總測試</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #ff6600;"><span style="color: #ff6600; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">7.完成</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;"><strong style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">實驗開始</span></strong></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #ff6600;">一.<span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">先用Realterm(終端機)測試ESP8266是否正常。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">跟初次上手應用那篇完全一樣喔，目的是測試ESP是否工作在9600的鮑率下，如果鮑率不是9600請點以下連結，留言的19樓有解決方法。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #ff6600;"><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><a style="text-decoration: none; color: #020202;" href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/blog/post/82031214"><span style="color: #0000ff; text-decoration: underline;">*教學*Arduino WIFI(ESP8266) 初次上手應用</span></a></span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #ff6600;">二.</span><span style="color: #ff6600;">再用Arduino取代Realterm(終端機)與ESP8266溝通，開內建序列監控室窗進行測試。<br><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px; color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">跟初次上手應用那篇</span>完全一樣喔，目的是測試你Arduino是否能燒錄(?)，ESP是否能溝通上，也是個簡單的測試指令方法。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #ff6600;"><a style="text-decoration: none; color: #020202;" href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/blog/post/82031214"><span style="color: #0000ff; text-decoration: underline;">*教學*Arduino WIFI(ESP8266) 初次上手應用</span></a></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #ff6600;"><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">三.輸入查找IP指令取得IP位址。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">1.網頁控制前提之下要先找到ESP的IP位址，所以要使用到"AT+CIFSR"指令查找，本篇建議就直接用Arduino下指令就好。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">&nbsp;<a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/17673721"><img title="查找IP.jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1450971585-2124949089.jpg" alt="查找IP.jpg" width="457" height="453" border="0"></a></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">2.如上圖，會有兩個IP位址，請把第二個(如圖172.20.10.8)複製下來，稍後在實驗5會用到。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">3.實驗三完成。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #ff6600;"><span style="color: #0000ff;"><span style="color: #ff6600; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">四.下載本實驗需要用到的檔案，並燒錄本實驗的code</span></span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #0000ff;"><span style="color: #000000;">1.<span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #0000ff;"><a href="https://drive.google.com/open?id=0B-cOA-yZ3-mDZHRmTG5OZGZKc2s"><span style="color: #0000ff; text-decoration: underline;">下載本實驗的檔案</span></a></span>，請整個資料夾一起抓下來。</span><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><br></span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #0000ff;"><span style="color: #000000;">2.打開"ESP8266_Wifi_Connection_Template.ino"，修改第5、6行的SSID、PASS，用你自己的數據機或手機熱點之使用者名稱、密碼。</span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">3.燒錄進Arduino，打開<span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">內建序列監控視窗，查看是否連上wifi，並接通port 8888，正確結果如下圖，<span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">實驗四完成</span></span>。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;"><a href="http://lolwarden.pixnet.net/album/photo/17675290"><img title="測試1.jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1450972570-3910299523.jpg" alt="測試1.jpg" width="443" height="426" border="0"></a></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #ff6600;"><span style="color: #ff6600;"><span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #ff6600; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">五.</span><span style="color: #ff6600; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">建構網頁，並修改html內碼。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">1.網頁的東西，基本上要學的很多，所以就不一一介紹，直接給已經編寫好的範例，再去修改是最快上手的方式。回到資料夾路徑下，有個網頁HTML檔"Esp8266 智慧控制.html"，如果點開會發現3個按鈕，稍後才會用到先關閉。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">2.接著要編輯裡面的內容，最直覺的方式是打開記事本，然後把HTML檔直接拉進去，如圖。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;"><img title="修改HTML.jpg" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1450972333-3707268487.jpg" alt="修改HTML.jpg" border="0"></span></p>

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">3.如圖，我們可以看到這個HTML已經可以任意編輯，而我們在實驗三有用<span style="font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">"AT+CIFSR"指令找到IP，就是要用在這邊，請把這行程式碼:</span></span></p>

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">1.控制端跟接收端都備齊了，接下來做最後的總測試。打開剛剛修改好的HTML檔，三個按鈕分別是:Pin11,12,13，如圖。</span></p>

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; line-height: 20px;"><span style="color: #000000;">原理很簡單，就是<span>當你點下按鈕時，HTML會發送你的IP網址最後再加上/PinXX到AP端，然後你的ESP8266就會收到一大串指令(有興趣可以用終端機去研究)，經過Arduino解碼，取得編碼最後的PinXX，然後對應你Arduino的Pin腳做控制，不過這個實驗只侷限在區域網路下喔，目前還在研究怎麼用網際網路來控制。</span></span></p>
