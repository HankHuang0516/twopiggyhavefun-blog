<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">台南的第一站我們來到「井仔腳瓦盤鹽田」</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">由瓦缸片鋪成的瓦盤鹽田，曬出來的鹽帶有顆粒狀又潔白，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">不論從哪個角度拍，一座座小山純白模樣都滿有療癒感!!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546726858" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1934/31546726858_e9d5606d14_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田01"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #ff6600;">井仔腳瓦盤鹽田</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">地址：臺南市北門區永華里井仔腳興安宮旁</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">開放時間：08:30～18:00</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">停車可停興安宮前廣場及路邊</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">「井仔腳瓦盤鹽田」是北門區第一座鹽田，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">於西元1818年建立，至今已有約200年的歷史，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">是現存最古老的鹽田遺址~~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546725818" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1953/31546725818_b9c87f4eef_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田02"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546725598" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1940/31546725598_cc65d32c67_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田03"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">附近停車還算便利，有提供免費停車場，而且這裡免門票唷~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">週邊也有販賣鹽製的食物，像是鹽滷蛋、加鹽仙草汁等…</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546725438" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1916/31546725438_897441f375_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田04"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546726458" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1965/31546726458_6ef63312b7_z.jpg" alt="IMG(27)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">拍照前可以先了解一下「井仔腳瓦盤鹽田」相關資訊~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">曬鹽其實就是個看天吃飯的行業，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">主要是因為台灣的乾雨季並不明顯，颱風又多~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">往往曬好的鹽可能因一場大雨就都溶化掉了….</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">推鹽 &gt; 獨輪車集鹽 &gt; 鹽粒成堆，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">看似美麗的鹽田，背後往往有著不為人知的辛勞….</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546726658" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1903/31546726658_b0bb768892_z.jpg" alt="IMG(19)"></a></span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546726168" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1975/31546726168_f3646201c7_z.jpg" alt="IMG(31)"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">當天去的時候天氣真的爆好耶~天空完全不用修XD</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">呈現棋盤狀的鹽田，堆疊像一座座小山，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">純白的鹽田讓人看的心情也愉悅~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546725038" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1908/31546725038_b407075a55_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田06"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">經由陽光照射所產生的絕美畫面，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">無論從哪個角度拍都相當迷人~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546724758" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1950/31546724758_ca87785260_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田07"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">太陽實在很熱情加上風又大，眼睛完全睜不開XD</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546724458" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1954/31546724458_1c9a50686c_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田08"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546724198" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1911/31546724198_6644dfc7cf_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田09"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546723968" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1970/31546723968_7397237d3b_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田10"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">有人跟我一樣會好奇</span><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #ff9900;">為什麼鹽田是堆成小山丘狀</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">嗎?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">來來來~~小豬幫大家查好了!!!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">曬好的鹽護衛堆在中間成一座小山丘狀，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">是方便雨水來襲時在上蓋布，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">以確保曬好的鹽不會被雨天沖刷掉喔!</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546723728" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1913/31546723728_5831afd315_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田11"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546723388" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1958/31546723388_5ee09acf52_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田12"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546723108" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1925/31546723108_93fcdb1a2c_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田13"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">一旁踩水車可體驗將小蒸發池的海水抽至鹵池，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">看小豬玩的不亦樂乎，哈哈</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546722798" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1949/31546722798_fb2bb7435e_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田15"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">往販賣部的方向走，還有一座鹽屋可以拍 ( 馬 上 躲 進 去 )</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546722468" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1980/31546722468_d663e8d2d6_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田17"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546722268" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1960/31546722268_396f5df41d_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田18"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">走進販賣部，主要販售鹽製產品，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">一旁有座位區可以坐下來休息~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546722058" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1979/31546722058_4117585858_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田19"></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546721798" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1963/31546721798_70b995d2dc_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田20"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">當天天氣實在太熱，我們買了一隻鹽製冰淇淋消暑一下XD</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">還特別拿到外面取景，不到3秒冰淇淋已經開始融化….</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">口味上其實跟想像中有落差，鹽味沒有特別明顯，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">小豬還說可以自己拿旁邊的鹽來調味= =”鋪…</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546722038" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1908/31546722038_9ddbef8977_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田21"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這個地中海風建築沒想到是個廁所，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt;">害我都不好意思站在外面拍XD</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/31546721658" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1936/31546721658_2ebc8b1428_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田22"></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">總結：</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">第一次造訪「井仔腳瓦盤鹽田」，看著一座座純白鹽田，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">經由陽光照射所產生的絕美畫面，無論從哪個角度拍都相當迷人~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">而這裡的鹽田是採用「瓦盤鹽田」的方式，將厚約6公厘的破缸片鋪面，</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">增加陽光的輻射吸收，曬出來的鹽相對顆粒細而潔白~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #008080;">很適合在好天氣時安排來這裡唷~</span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/158881690@N04/30482088217" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm2.staticflickr.com/1950/30482088217_1fe76e7222_z.jpg" alt="井仔腳瓦盤鹽田23"></a></p>
