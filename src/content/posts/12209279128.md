<p><span style="letter-spacing: 1px; font-size: 16px; line-height: 20px; font-family: 新細明體; color: #0000ff;">**喜歡旅遊、</span><span style="letter-spacing: 1px; font-size: 16px; line-height: 20px; font-family: 新細明體; color: #0000ff;">愛吃美食嗎？歡迎加入<span style="color: #ff0000;">&nbsp;<span style="text-decoration: underline;"><a style="color: #000000; text-decoration: underline;" href="https://www.facebook.com/twopiggyhavefun/?fref=ts" target="_blank"><span style="color: #ff0000; text-decoration: underline;">兩隻小豬粉絲團</span></a></span></span>**</span><span style="color: #777777; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px; font-size: 12pt;"><img title="IMG_20160925_234157_402" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1475306460-1846871428.jpg" alt="IMG_20160925_234157_402" border="0">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #008000;"><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">望古瀑布</span></strong></span><br><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">地址:新北市平溪區慶和橋26號(望古車站旁)</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">趁著假日好天氣，兩隻小豬來到平溪走走~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">其實每次來平溪，大多都是逛平溪老街、菁桐老街、十分瀑布...等</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">這次想說來點不一樣的景點~剛好在網路看到有人分享</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">望古車站旁的「望古瀑布」</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">往一旁的步道上去，岩層中有處傾瀉而下的"望古瀑布"</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: arial; letter-spacing: 1px;"><img title="IMG_20160925_234355_296" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1475307010-193984787.jpg" alt="IMG_20160925_234355_296" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160925_235433_219" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1475307010-24947245.jpg" alt="IMG_20160925_235433_219" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160925_235428_533" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1475307010-453518383.jpg" alt="IMG_20160925_235428_533" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">逛完望古步道，也可以到<strong>嶺腳</strong>走走</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">嶺腳過去因為煤業發展曾經風華一時</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">現今雖已繁華不再，仍留下許多產業遺跡，供人探索玩味</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160925_235411_476" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1475307010-2765442462.jpg" alt="IMG_20160925_235411_476" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">走到這邊，會先看到小瀑布，還不是<span style="font-size: 16px;">望古瀑布喔</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">望古瀑布共有4層，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">沿著步道從下而上分為一瀑和二瀑</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">至於三瀑和四瀑目前尚未完整的開發，只能從樹林的縫隙一窺究竟</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160925_233843_877" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1475307010-166317356.jpg" alt="IMG_20160925_233843_877" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160925_233907_395" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1475307010-4238853026.jpg" alt="IMG_20160925_233907_395" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">要抵達觀賞瀑布的地方，步行約10分鐘左右就可以到</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">假日也有不少遊客來觀賞望谷瀑布的美景</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160925_234008_156" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1475307010-570924248.jpg" alt="IMG_20160925_234008_156" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">沿著棧道往上走，就是望古<span style="font-size: 16px;">瀑布啦~~</span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">雖然沒有像十分瀑布壯觀，</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">不過可以感受到山林間瀑布的清幽，簡直是忘憂小秘境<img src="//s.pixfs.net/f.pixnet.net/images/emotions/shades_smile.gif" alt="" border="0"></span></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160925_234157_402" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1475307010-1189154269.jpg" alt="IMG_20160925_234157_402" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">望古瀑布的湖光山色隱身於山谷之內，像是與世隔絕的世外桃源</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">底下的水非常清澈，讓人想脫下鞋子下去走走XD</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; color: #000000;">不過這裡的水還是有點深度，大家別輕易嘗試唷</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"><img title="IMG_20160925_234219_613" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1475307010-3025853608.jpg" alt="IMG_20160925_234219_613" border="0"></span></p>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">大家來到望谷瀑布，記得穿雙好走的鞋子，這樣比較安全~</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">如果你也喜歡尋古訪幽，不妨來到望古瀑布裡，一同感受山林間瀑布的清幽~</span></p>
<p><img title="IMG_20160925_234000_975" src="https://pimg.1px.tw/lolwarden/1475307010-2496107469.jpg" alt="IMG_20160925_234000_975" border="0"></p>
